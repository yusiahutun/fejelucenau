Travis Kelce's ex turns 'men into stone' as he gets 'serious' with Taylor Swift

Publish date: 2024-03-27

Travis Kelce’s ex is pulling out her ‘A-Game’ amid the ‘Traylor’ media blitz. After sharing that she is prepared to deal with the ‘worst’ that is to come, the fashion influencer is channeling her ‘goddess’ aura in her latest Instagram post.

NFL heartthrob Travis Kelce‘s ex Kayla Nicole was in an on-and-off relationship with the Kansas City Chiefs player for almost half a decade. Reports claim the celebrity couple started dating in 2017 and initially split in 2020 but got back together eventually. Nicole and Kelce finally called it quits in 2022 which led to the latter to allegedly needing ‘therapy.’

Travis Kelce’s ex jokes she turns ‘men into stone’

Amidst headlines of Travis and Taylor in every news outlet, the NFL celebrity‘s ex is showing that she is living her best life.

The social media influencer decked up as Medusa for Halloween donning a racy leather black and gold bodysuit with a shimmering sarong skirt and knee-high leather boots.

The Pepperdine University completed her look with gold accessories, featuring a headpiece that resembles snakes, very Reputation-ish, and paired it with hoops, bracelets, and rings.

The caption of her smokey Instagram post read: “Medusa the Greek goddess was categorically known to turn men to stone with one look…..I can relate.”

“I had never felt so bad in my life”

Amid the excitement of Travis starting a new chapter with Taylor Swift where reports claim it is getting serious, his ex Kayla Nicole shared what it took for her to heal from the split.

The fashion influencer who often uses her Instagram as a platform to speak her truth talked about being thankful for going into therapy after her breakup.

“I’m posting content and people are like, ‘You look so good. Tell us your tricks. You look amazing.’ And I’m like, ‘I’m miserable. I’m miserable. I’m depressed,'” Nicole confessed.

She added: “My abs were amazing, I had a six-pack. And it was like, ‘I had never felt so bad in my life.'”

Nicole also emphasized the importance of therapy saying: “So, I started going to therapy and quickly realized, okay, it’s one thing to be physically fit, but it’s another thing to be mentally fit. You could be in the best shape of your life, but mentally, if you are not a healthy person, it’s not going to feel good.”

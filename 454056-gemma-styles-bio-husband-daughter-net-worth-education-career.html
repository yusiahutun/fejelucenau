Early life

Gemma was born on 3 December 1990 (age 31) in Evesham, Worcestershire, England. She is the daughter of Desmond Styles (father) and Anne Twist (mother). Her father worked as a finance worker, and her mother worked as a landlady.

Gemma is the oldest child in the family, and her siblings are Harry Styles, Mike Twist, and Amy. However, Mike is her stepbrother. Gemma Styles’ parents divorced in 2001 when she was ten. As a result, her mother married her business partner John Cox and took his name.

She became Anne Cox but dropped the name Cox after her marriage to John ended. Anne then married a man named Robin Twist on 1 June 2013 and inherited Twist's name, hence Anne Twist. Also, Anne and Robin welcomed a child called Mike Twist, Gemma’s stepbrother.

Gemma Styles grew up in Holmes Chapel, Cheshire, England. She attended Holmes Chapel Comprehensive School together with his biological brother Harry. After high school, Gemma joined Sheffield Hallam University, a public research university in Sheffield, South Yorkshire, England.

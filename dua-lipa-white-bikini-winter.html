Dua Lipa Wore a White Bikini in a Hot Tub

Publish date: 2024-06-03

Dua Lipa is bracing the cold in your not-so-typical winter outfit: a bikini. On Sunday, the singer shared a photo dump from her cozy-looking girls trip to the countryside.

In the IG gallery, Dua shared pictures with her gals doing facials, petting a horse, and taking a dip in a steaming hot tub. She also included a few selfies in a bright red jacket worn over a crisp white button down with a navy blue Prada tie and jeans. She accessorized with white fuzzy Hello Kitty boot slippers.

"country reset," she captioned the carousel. For the jacuzzi portion of the excursion, the singer opted for a very tiny white, bustier-style bathing suit. One video showed her easing into the hot water, while another photo captured her and her friend posing in matching suits.

Earlier this month, the stylish pop star had to cancel a performance due to a case of laryngitis. She explained the cancellation and apologized to her fans via social media. "As most of you know I'm currently suffering laryngitis and have been on vocal rest per my doctor's orders," she wrote on Instagram. "I was hoping for a faster recovery, but unfortunately, i am still under the weather and unable to perform at the iHeartRadio Z100 Jingle Bell Ball."

"I am extremely disappointed," she concluded. "I was looking forward to kicking off the holiday season with my fans."

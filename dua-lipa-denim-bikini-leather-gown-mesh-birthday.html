Dua Lipa Wore 3 Dramatically Risqu Outfits for Her Birthday

Publish date: 2024-02-25

In typical Leo fashion, Dua Lipa is delivering one dazzling look after the other in honor of her 27th birthday.

The Grammy-winning pop star recently embarked on a late-summer trip to ring in her milestone, sharing multiple photos of her showstopping outfits on Instagram along the way. In some of her most recent posts, Lipa shared a peek at three separate striking birthday girl outfits.

First, the "Sweetest Pie" singer opted for a head-to-toe vintage look, wearing a jean-print bikini and matching sarong from Dior's spring 2000 collection. For shoes, she wore a pair of monogrammed lace-up stiletto boots, also from the French fashion house's early-aughts runway.

She blended gold and silver with her accessories, wearing silver Bottega Veneta chain earrings, a silver lily choker from Hugo Kreit, a gold ring from Spinelli Kilcollin, and another chunky gold ring from Aurem. Lipa capped off the look with yellow-tinted shield sunnies from Kaleos Edwards.

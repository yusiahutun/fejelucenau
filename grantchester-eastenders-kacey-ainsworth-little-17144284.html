EastEnders' Little Mo actress Kacey Ainsworth, 48, stuns in plunging jumpsuit

Publish date: 2024-07-06

Kacey Ainsworth, who played 'Little' Mo Slater on EastEnders, rose to fame after appearing in the soap from 2000 until 2006.

The actress, who now stars as Cathy Keating in ITV drama Grantchester, owned the red carpet in a low-cut jumpsuit.

Attending the Gold Movie Awards at its second annual independent film awards ceremony, Kacey looked stunning in the sparkly outfit.

The beauty posed for photographers with her ample cleavage on show.

Showcasing a new short hair 'do, Kacey looked beaming with happiness.

She paired the jumpsuit with nude kitten heels and opted for large hope earrings.

Going au naturale, Kacey wore a nude lip with eyeliner and some blush to add understated glamour.

Sadly for Little Mo fans, Kacey admitted on Rylan Clark-Neal’s BBC Sounds podcast that she has ruled herself out of returning to EastEnders.

“My problem is I’m too busy. Every time we’ve had a conversation about it I’ve gone and got another job,” she admitted.

“Then I’m not available for the year. I would love us to all get back together again.”

Although, she did admit that she’s still close with her fellow Slaters, and that a reunion isn’t entirely off the cards.

“My onscreen family live in my heart all of them,” she said.

“We had our rows don’t get me wrong, we shouted at each other like a proper family, but I am in contact with Jessie [Kat Moon] and Elaine [Lynn Hobbs] and Michelle [Zoe Slater], and Mo [Laila Morse] and I are at the same agency.

“They live in my heart because we went through so much together.”

Paige Spiranac says her raunchy OnlyPaige snaps are 'too aggressive' for Instagram

Publish date: 2024-03-10

Paige Spiranac explained that her OnlyFans pictures are too aggressive to be on social media when fans asked why they have to pay for content.

Golf beauty Spiranac has amassed a huge collective of 3.7million followers on Instagram where she regularly wows them with scintillating pictures showing off her body. But the steamy shots left fans wanting to see more, leading to the creation of the blonde's OnlyPaige website, a spin-off from adult content site OnlyFans.

Spiranac launched the site last week after being bombarded by supporters to do so. And they'll have to pay $9.99 per month or $99.99 a year to view Spiranac's exclusive images and videos that don't include any nudity.

Who is Andrea D Brooks? Explore her Biography, Facts and Peronal Life

Publish date: 2024-10-03

Andrea D Brooks is an American YouTuber as well as a lifestyle coach. She is widely famous for her fashion tips and do-it-yourself videos which she frequently uploads to her YouTube channel AndreasChoice.

Early Life of a YouTuber

Andrea D Brooks opened her eyes on 4th September 1986 in Roseville, California, the United States of America. As of 2022, her age is 36 years old while her horoscope is Virgo. Her birth name is Andrea Danielle Brooks. Her mother’s name is Melissa Brooks. She grew up with her twin sister named Brittany Brooks and a brother named Anthony Brooks in her hometown, California.

Andrea holds an American nationality and belongs to a white ethnical background. Brooks had an interest in art as a child and often illustrated her own comic books. It was during her high school days that Andrea became interested in makeup and fashion.

Career

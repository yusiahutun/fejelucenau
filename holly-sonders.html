What is Holly Sonders’ net worth? Who is Holly Sonders dating?

Introduction

Holly Sonders is an American sportscaster, social media personality, and a former Fox Sports reporter.

Biography

She was born on March 3, 1987, in Marysville, Ohio, USA, as Holly Niederkohr.

Sonders played basketball and baseball on boys’ teams until high school.

In 2009, Holly graduated from Michigan State. During college, she competed for the women’s golf team. 

In 2007, Sonders reached the round of 16 at the Women’s Western Amateur.

”I began playing golf tournaments when I was 6 years old and was burned out on it by the time I hit high school,” she said in 2020. ”I continued to play through college.”

”My mom, Sandy, has been club champion for 22 years,” Sonders told Golf Digest. ”She got me started when I was 6.” 

Unfortunately, Holly suffered a knee injury while playing collegiate golf.

Golf Channel

Holly Sonders attends the special screening of the HBO original documentary “The Golden Boy” at The GRAMMY Museum on July 17, 2023 in Los Angeles – @Getty

In 2011, Sonders got a job at the Golf Channel.

In May 2013, she landed herself on the cover of Golf Digest.

In July 2014, she left the Golf Channel for Fox.

In 2015, she made a guest appearance on the TV series ”New Girl.”

In July 2015, Sonders was reassigned by Fox prior to this week’s U.S. Women’s Open. ”I think Holly’s been great for us. It’s just that she can’t do both the postgame show and the interviews,” Mark Loomis, Fox’s coordinating producer, told USA Today.

In October 2018, Fox Sports announced that Sonders would no longer be a part of its golf broadcasts.

”The golf at Fox just didn’t turn out to be what we thought it would be,” Sonders stated. She added: ”We hoped we would get more PGA Tour rights, and it just didn’t end up happening.”

Later, Holly started contributing to the network’s NFL coverage.

© Guliver / Getty Images

She left FOX Sports in October 2019.

”After 10 years of it, and the same golf crowd, I just wanted to live my life,” she said.

In May 2020, Sonders has unveiled an app called TEAMUP Fitness that has developed a unique feature called the “Marketplace” where a Fitness Pro can offer LIVE Experiences.

In December 2020, Holly announced that she was starting her version of OnlyFans, a website called hollysonders.com.

Husband

In May 2012, Holly Sonders got engaged to Erik Kuselias, a radio and television personality who works for CBS Sports.

The two divorced in 2016.

In early 2019, she started to date Arizona Cardinals coach Kliff Kingsbury.

Fiancé

In December 2019, Sonders revealed that she is engaged to Vegas Dave (real name Dave Oancea),  a former professional sports gambler who refers to himself as ‘#1 sports information consultant.’

@Getty

They met through Instagram after exchanging messages with each other.

”I was so taken by Dave’s personal story and his rise above adversity to become a self-made man that I sincerely fell in love with him instantly,” Holly said.

”I never thought this day would ever happen,” Dave wrote on Instagram. ”God only knows Holly how much I have searched for a partner like you.”

It is unclear if Holly and Oancea are still together.

In August 2020, Dave said: ”Me and Holly are still together, but for the last month and a half, I’ve moved back in with my parents so I can sleep good at night.”

Update – the two broke up.

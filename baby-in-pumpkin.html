14 Baby in Pumpkin Pictures

Publish date: 2024-07-20
1

Go Big!

Adriana Cavazos / EyeEm

The key to a successful photo of a baby in a pumpkin is starting with a big pumpkin! This little one looks perfectly comfy in this oversize gourd with holes cut for his feet.

2

Add a Blanket

Getty Images

Even tiny babies can get in on the Halloween fun. Protect his delicate skin (and keep him mess-free!) by linking the pumpkin with a soft blanket.

3

Take It Outside

Aaron Black

This little one looks perfectly happy in his pumpkin perch! Take the photo outside where a blanket of fall leaves can provide a seasonal backdrop.

4

Keep It Simple

Fertnig

All you really need is a happy baby and a hollowed-out pumpkin for an adorable photo your Instagram followers will love!

5

Be Flexible

kokouu

Of course, not every baby is happy to be put in a pumpkin for a photo shoot. Have a cute costume on hand as a backup plan!

6

Add an Accessory

c3nsored

This little one's cute felt pumpkin barrette adds the perfect touch to this photo!

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

7

Knit a Handmade Topper

Alitsiya

If you have knitting skills, make a sweet hat for your baby to wear. You're sure to use it throughout the fall season!

8

Celebrate the Milestone

Getty

Add a "baby's first Halloween" chalkboard with age, weight, number of teeth, and add her favorites, like a lullaby and storybook. You'll cherish this memory for years to come!

9

Get Creative with Carving

Tatyana Tomsickova Photography

Sure, a plain pumpkin is cute enough, but add some clever carving for an extra-cute photo. This one has a silly face with an orange fabric tongue.

Advertisement - Continue Reading Below

10

Act Fast

Johnny Schafer / EyeEm

You're likely working with a short time frame while your baby indulges your photo shoot, so be ready with a background and pumpkin, and then put the baby inside for a few quick snaps.

11

Color Coordinate

Crystal Bolin Photography

This little girl has a coordinating orange dress that blends in perfectly with the pumpkin. Her seasonal bow is a darling finishing touch!

12

Keep It Inside

by John Carleton

If the weather is cold or uncooperative, you'll want to keep your naked baby warm inside! Find an unadorned spot in your like an unlit fireplace to serve as a backdrop. Fill in with more pumpkins or other seasonal decor.

13

Go With Clothes

ullstein bild

Not all babies are happy getting messy, and most parents don't want an unnecessary bath on their schedule! Comfy pajamas in a seasonal color are the perfect solution to keep your baby clean and happy.

14

No Baby? No Problem

summersetretrievers

A puppy in a pumpkin is just as cute!

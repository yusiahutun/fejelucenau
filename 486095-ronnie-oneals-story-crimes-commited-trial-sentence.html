Who is Ronnie O'Neal?

Ronnie O'Neal III is a convicted felon. His actions left many mouths agape. He murdered the mother of his two children, his daughter, and attempted to kill his second child.

How old is Ronnie O'Neal?

The convicted criminal is 35 years old as of 2024. He was born on 27 July 1988, and his Zodiac sign is Leo.

Where is Ronnie O'Neal from?

The convicted felon was born and raised in Florida, United States of America. His nationality is American, and his ethnicity is African-American.

Is Ronnie O'Neal still alive?

Yes, the convicted felon is still alive. He is serving time in prison at the Blackwater River Correctional Facility in Milton, Santa Rosa County, Florida. Before his arrest, trial, and sentencing, he lived in Riverview in Hillsborough County, Florida.

Childhood

Ronnie O'Neal was brought up in a difficult environment. He did not know his biological father until adulthood. He was raised by his stepdad, Billy Smith. He has a brother named Alonso Kelley.

What did Ronnie O'Neal do?

Ronnie O'Neal murdered his ex-girlfriend, Kenyatta Barron and his nine-year-old daughter. He also attempted to murder his son.

His son's name is Ronnie O'Neal IV, and the name of his deceased daughter is Ron'Niveya O'Neal. He fatally shot Kenyatta Barron and hacked Ron'Niveya to death with an axe. He also stabbed Ronnie O'Neal IV, who survived.

The murders are commonly known as the Riverview murders. Ronnie O'Neal’s son told the detectives that his father shot his mother before being taken to a local hospital for immediate medical attention.

The Riverview murders occurred on 18 March 2018 in Riverview, Florida, United States of America, where the convicted felon lived, not far from Kenyatta Barron and their two kids.

Ronnie O'Neal's family

Kenyatta Barron was born on 13 November 1984. She fell in love and moved in with Ronnie O'Neal. The two welcomed their first child, a daughter named Ron'Niveya, in 2009.

Ron'Niveya was born with health challenges. Doctors diagnosed her with cerebral palsy and autism, so she could not speak. She used a wheelchair and sign language to communicate.

The two welcomed their second child, a son named Ronnie O’Neal IV, in 2011. In 2012, Kenyatta Barron filed a paternity petition in a family court to determine her son’s biological father.

The court determined that Ronnie O’Neal III was Ronnie O’Neal IV's biological father. In 2016, Barron and her boyfriend decided to break up. They separated and lived apart. The convicted felon moved on and welcomed a son named Iesha Robinson with his new girlfriend.

Ronnie O'Neal's evidence, trial, and sentencing

On 16 June 2021, O’Neal’s trial began. The court allowed him to serve as his own attorney in addition to a counsel. He defended himself when he went on trial and accused the authorities of setting him up.

His son testified as the prosecution witness, answering his father, "You stabbed me," during cross-examination at a Hillsborough County court.

The relevant authorities conducted autopsies on Kenyatta Barron and her daughter and presented them before the court as evidence.

It was revealed that Kenyatta Barron suffered a gunshot wound on her right back shoulder and another gunshot wound on her right elbow, where actual pellets from the shotgun were recovered.

Her right arm was broken in two separate places. The officials revealed that these wounds would probably not have been lethal by themselves.

It was further revealed that the late Kenyatta Barron suffered multiple injuries to her head, including over 15 separate lacerations, multiple abrasions, and contusions, mainly on the left side.

Did Ronnie O’Neal III have a mental illness?

A forensic psychologist who testified at O’Neal’s trial said that the shooting and childhood abuse led O’Neal to develop post-traumatic stress disorder and delusional disorder. The mental issues may have contributed to his heinous crime.

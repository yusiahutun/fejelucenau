Eva Mendes poses in a swimsuit on sunny family vacation with Ryan Gosling and daughters

Publish date: 2024-02-10

Eva Mendes is making the best of her weekend, getting some vacation time at a beachside locale, presumably with her partner Ryan Gosling and their two daughters.

The actress shared several photographs from their getaway, some of which saw her pose in a swimsuit with a variety of cover ups or scarves.

Her pictures highlighted her natural beauty and her penchant for colorful styling, even sporting varied head wraps and flower crowns in her photos, peppered with shots of their pool and the beach with its own lavender haze.

"This Cubanita always finds el sol [transl: the sun]," she captioned her compilation of photos, quickly receiving a barrage of compliments.

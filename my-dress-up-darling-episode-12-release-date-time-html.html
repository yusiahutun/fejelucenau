'My Dress-Up Darling' Episode 12 Release Date and Time for the Season 1 Finale

After episode 11 dropped, fans want to know the ‘My Dress-Up Darling’  Episode 12 release date, time, and where to watch the next chapter for Wakana Gojo and Marin Kitagawa.

Published on March 23, 2022

2 min read

One of the best romance anime currently running is My Dress-Up Darling (Japanese name Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo Suru). Marin Kitagawa and Gojo share moments of love and hilarious awkwardness. Of course, a romance series makes fans wait as the couple inch closer to confessing their feelings in My Dress-Up Darling Episode 12. However, Kitagawa might want to hurry up, as in previous episodes, it seems Juju likes Gojo, too.

  • When to watch the My Dress-Up Darling Episode 12 release date and time.
  • My Dress-Up Darling Episode 11 recap

‘My Dress-Up Darling’ Episode 12 release date and time

The new episode is titled “My Dress-Up Darling,” and it will release on Saturday, March 26, 2022, or Sunday, March 27, 2022, based on your location. You can watch new episodes of the Winter 2022 anime on Crunchyroll and Funimation’s official website.

Episode 12 of My Dress-Up Darling will follow different time schedules for different locations.

  • 8:00 am PT (March 26)
  • 10:00 am CT (March 26)
  • 11:00 am EST (March 26)
  • 4:00 pm GMT (March 26)
  • 12:00 am JST (March 27)

However, only viewers with premium Crunchyroll or Funimation subscriptions can catch My Dress-Up Darling episode 12 at these times.

If you only have a free account, though, you’ll have to wait a week to see the new episodes. Those should then release on April 1, 2022.

My Dress-Up Darling is not available for streaming on Netflix or Hulu, in Japanese or English dub. Crunchyroll and Funimation remain the two most reliable places to catch new episodes of the show.

‘Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo Suru’ Episode 11 recap

Before you watch Episode 12 of My Dress-Up Darling, here’s a recap episode 11 .

Marin Kitagawa and Wakana Gojo headed out to take new cosplay pictures. However, Kitagawa booked a love hotel for the shoot. Of course, it led to some hilariously awkward situations.

Kitagawa tried out a new costume based on a succubus monster. Even though she said she would be more careful about choosing her outfits, this one also exposed as much skin as her bikini.

However, after Gojo tried shooting a specific angle, he and Kitagawa found themselves face to face. It looked like Gojo and Kitagawa would finally kiss. They came tantalizingly close before the inevitable interruption.

With My Dress-Up Darling Episode 12 being the season 1 finale, fans hope the couple makes some kind of progress.

With ‘My Dress-Up Darling’ Episode 12 release date, fans hope for a season 2

As season 1 ends, fans want to know if there will be a My Dress-Up Darling Season 2.

While CloverWorks has not announced anything about My Dress-Up Darling being canceled or renewed, it has proved to be one of the most popular anime for Winter 2022.

And with more volumes of the My Dress-Up Darling manga still not adapted, the anime has plenty of material for a second season.

Fans can watch the season finale of My Dress-Up Darling on Saturday, March 26, 2022, or Sunday, March 27, 2022, on Crunchyroll and Funimation.

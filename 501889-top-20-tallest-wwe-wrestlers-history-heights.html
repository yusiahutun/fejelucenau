Top 20 tallest WWE wrestlers in history and their heights

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Most of the tallest WWE wrestlers are legends and obscure but are/were fun to watch. Besides being famous for their sheer strength and size, these wrestlers have a rare combination of skills, making them vicious.

To make it to WWE and win major titles, one must be skilled, tall, and young. Pro wrestling has featured several enormous men in its rings. Some of these wrestlers are so large to seem real. There have been professional wrestlers who are over 7 ft. tall and beyond 400 lbs. However, being tall and skilled has offered most wrestlers massive advantages over their opponents.

Tallest WWE wrestlers

WWE has featured several tall wrestlers, but according to The Sportster, these are the tallest in history.

1. Giant Gonzalez - 8’0”

Giant Gonzalez is the only 8 ft. wrestler ever featured for WWE. The former basketball player had colorful years in WWE in the 90s. Clad in a furry, full-body jumpsuit, Giant Gonzalez was the tallest wrestler in the world and one of the physically imposing players.

2. Andre the Giant - 7’4”

Andre Rene Roussinoff, popular as Andre the Giant, was 7 ft. 4 inches tall and the second largest wrestler. Andre the Giant had a successful career and some significant wins, the most famous being against Hulk Hogan during the 80s.

However, most people that watched him mostly spoke of his gregarious personality. Andre had a great attitude toward his friends and was highly competitive.

He was regarded as the 8th wonder of the world, which made him seem like the tallest and largest man to ever walk on Earth. But in terms of WWE stars, he only ranked as second.

3. Omos - 7’3”

Tolulope Omogbehin, a 28-year-old Nigerian athlete, is the youngest player on this list. Omos stands at 7 ft. 3 inches tall and weighs 183 kg. His first appearance in WWE was in June 2020, Monday Night Raw. He was a member of Akira Tozawa's Ninja group.

4. Giant Silva - 7’2’’

Paulo Cesar da Silva is a forgotten big man. The former Brazilian basketball player was 7 ft. 2 inches tall. He was nicknamed Giant Silva in WWE and was an infamous Oddities team member.

However, he competed on the losing end of handicap matches and the tag team. Unfortunately, Giant Silva didn't stay in WWE for long and left little mark than other prominent men of his time.

5. The Great Khali - 7’1”

Dalip Singh Rana, popular as The Great Khali, can't miss the list of the tallest WWE wrestlers. The Indian star is 7ft. 1 inch tall, and his huge frame won him the World Heavyweight Championship in 2007.

6. Jackson Andrews - 7’1”

Jackson Andrews featured for FCW from 2009 to 2011, and his colossal frame was his primary asset. He was particularly rigid and thus didn't move quite swiftly while in the ring. 15. The Undertaker - 6’10”

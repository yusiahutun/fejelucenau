Salma Hayek praised for letting white hair 'shine' in 'naturally beautiful' selfies

Publish date: 2024-03-28

Salma Hayek has been praised by her Instagram followers for ‘gracefully embracing’ her white hair in a range of natural selfies.

The 56-year-old Hollywood actress burst onto the scene almost three decades ago, and fans have been stunned by her looks and acting skills ever since.

Salma Hayek ’embraces’ white hair

Taking to her almost 26 million Instagram followers, the 56-year-old posted a few selfies showing off the white streaks in her hair.

In the caption, she joked: “Here is a tip to cover your white hair without dyeing it………don’t wear your glasses 👓 on your hair!!!. Bring on the wisdom.”

Salma posed with little to no makeup in close-up selfies taken in the car, embracing her natural hair color with a proud smile on her face.

Fans praise the star in the comments

As soon as the star posted snaps of her ‘new hairdo’ fans rushed to the comments to praise her for ’embracing’ her whites.

The actress has been gracing her followers with bikini pics as of late, showing she can really ‘switch’ the content up, and it seems like fans are loving them both.

While another commented: “Natural beauty”

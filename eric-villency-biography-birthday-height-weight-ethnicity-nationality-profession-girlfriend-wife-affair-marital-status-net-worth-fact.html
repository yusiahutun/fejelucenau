Short Description on Eric Villency

Eric Villency is the ex-husband of American news personality and former attorney Kimberly Guilfoyle. He is the CEO of Villency Design Group since 1998 which is an interior and product design firm.

Furthermore, Eric Villency was born in New York City, the United States of America on 10th June 1975. He was born as the mixed of artist Rowan and chairman of Maurice Villency, Robert Villency. He completed his graduated from the University of Wisconsin.

Eric Villency Career

Villency’s grandfather, Maurice Villency, was the person to inaugurate the furniture company “Villency Design Group” back in 1932. Back in 1998, Eric was named as the CEO of the company. In 2001, Eric launched “Maurice Villency flagship store” that was based in midtown of Manhattan. Later, he also launched his first accessories collection.

Later, in 2006, he launched a workshop “VillencyAelier” that was dedicated to the entire professional designer. After two years, he started a new business for the business service “Villency Contract”. Moreover, with the starting of a new business, he promoted his several design products. He even contributed to the SoulCycle development which is an indoor fitness cycle.

Moreover, besides his designing career, he is a Television personality. He is famous for appearing as the guest on the shoes like “Today Show”, “Good Morning America”, “Travel Channel”, “The Tyra Banks” show and more. He was also the host of the Fine Living Channel’s iDesign. Recently, he produced and hosted the documentary INSEAM that records the emerging fashion designers.

Villency is a former model who maintains a connection to the fashion world. Moreover, he designed the Mercedes-Benz Star Lounge at the New York’s Fashion Week in February 2009 and is liked and praised by many.

Eric Villency Personal Life

Interestingly, Eric Villency is married twice and divorced once. He was previously married to Kimberly Guilfoyle. The couple tied the knot in 2006 and after spending only three years together the couple parted away in 2009. Additionally, the couple together has a child, a son, Ronan Anthony. After an amicable split in the year 2009, the couple now shares joint custody of the son Ronan.

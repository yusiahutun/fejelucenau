Meet Megan Fox's Mom Gloria Darlene Fox

Publish date: 2024-05-11

If there’s one thing Hollywood celebrities have in common, it has to be fans who are curious to know their roots. Gloria Darlene Fox is the mother of famous Hollywood Actress and model Megan Fox. If you’ve seen movies like “Transformers” and the “Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles” franchise, then you know who Megan Fox is. 

Megan is a successful actress who became a household name after her captivating portrayal of her role as Mikaela Banes in the blockbuster action film, “Transformers.” Since her breakout role, Megan Fox has won several awards and nominations, including two Scream Awards and four Teen Choice Awards.

As expected, fans soon turned their attention to Megan Fox’s background, eager to know all they could about her family. Many were, however, curious about the identity of Megan Fox’s mother, Gloria Darlene Fox, and whether she was a celebrity like her daughter. 

Who Is Megan Fox’s Mother?

Megan Fox’s mother, Gloria Darlene Fox, was born Gloria Darlene Cisson on July 14, 1952. Gloria was born in Cooksville, Tennessee, and is a white American of Irish descent. The famous actress’s mother has kept details of her early life private, and there is little information about her upbringing.

Gloria has neither said anything about having siblings nor mentioned her parents. However, we can confirm that she grew up in a Christian home and follows the faith to date. After high school, Gloria attended the Tennessee Technological University in Cooksville. Her course of study or year of graduation remains undisclosed.

She rose to fame as Megan Fox’s mom. However, she does not have an online presence, a trait her daughter seems to have. Despite being one of the most talked-about actresses in Hollywood, Megan Fox has zero pictures on her Instagram account.

While Gloria Darlene Fox, on the other hand, does not have an Instagram account. Although her daughter is famous, the 70-year-old mother prefers to live quietly and is rarely seen in the spotlight.

Is Megan Fox’s Mom an Actress?

No! Unlike her celebrity daughter, Gloria Darlene Fox was a real estate agent. After she graduated from college, Gloria landed her first job in the real estate industry in May 2008. The stunning actress’s mother worked at Marco Shores Estate, a real estate firm where she worked as a manager for a year.

Afterward, she joined the Imperial Bonita Estates and worked for six years before moving to Riverside Club Condominium Association in 2015. At Riverside, Gloria was the company’s general manager for a year. Eight years and three real estate companies later, Gloria moved to eXp Realty in 2019, where she worked as a sales specialist. 

Gloria Darlene Fox Has Been in Two Marriages

Megan Fox’s mom has been in two marriages her entire life. While one failed, the other wasn’t so great either. Her first husband was Franklin Thomas Fox, a parole officer in the United States. Details regarding how they met are unknown. However, reports claim it was love at first sight for the young couple, who were teenagers then.

The now-divorced couple hit it off and wasted no time in moving swiftly. They eventually tied the knot in 1971 and remained a couple for years before calling it quits in 1989. The reason for their divorce remains undisclosed. However, Gloria Darlene Fox moved on from the divorce and remarried years later.

Her second marriage to Tony Tonahio wasn’t a great one. Gloria Darlene Fox’s second husband was a conservative Pentecostal Christian 19 years older than her. Their marriage was tough on Gloria and especially on Megan, a kid at the time.

From being unable to bring friends home from school to not having a regular childhood, Megan left home to fend for herself. Gloria’s marriage to Tony lasted till 2011 when he passed away. 

Gloria Darlene Fox Has Two Daughters and Five Grandkids 

Aside from Megan, Gloria had another child from her first marriage, Kristi Michelle Fox. Kristi is her first daughter, and she gave birth to her in June 1974. Unlike her sister, Kristi works as a counselor in a school. She is married to Douglas Branim, a school coach.

In addition to having two daughters, Gloria Darlene Fox is blessed with five grandchildren. Kristi and her husband gave birth to two sons, Kyler and Caleb Branim. Megan also has three kids, Noah, Bodhi, and Journey Green, from her previous relationship with fellow actor Brian Austin Green.

Gloria Darlene Fox Has a Great Relationship With Megan Fox

A mother’s bond with her child is usually unbreakable, and Gloria has an unbreakable bond with her daughter, Megan. The bond became stronger when she started raising her as a single mom.

Gloria and her daughter are often photographed together on dates, and she is Megan’s confidant. Also, she knew about Megan’s Relationship with Machine Gun Kelly before he proposed to her. 

Gloria Darlene Fox is not the only celebrity mom who became famous thanks to her daughter’s success as an actress. Natalie Ortega is also known for her daughter’s success in Hollywood.

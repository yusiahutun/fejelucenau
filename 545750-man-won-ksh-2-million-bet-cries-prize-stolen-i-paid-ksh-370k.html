Man Who Won Over KSh 2 Million Bet Cries Out as His Prize is Stolen: I Was Only Paid KSh 370k

Publish date: 2024-04-08
  • A man who won over KSh 2 million after predicting 37 matches correctly has cried out for help to get paid
  • According to him, he has only received KSh 370,000 payment out of the vast amount he won despite involving the court
  • Social media users shared their thoughts about the situation, with many accusing the agent of fleeing with the cash

A man named Lukman Teriba cried out after getting a mere KSh 370,000 out of the KSh 2 million he won.

Lukman revealed that he predicted 37 games with KSh 120 to win KSh 2 million, but when he took his ticket to the betting shop, he was told the winnings had been withdrawn.

Lukman receives only KSh 370k payment

Speaking to PUNCH, the heartbroken man said he took the case to the Bet9ja head office, where the management urged their agent to pay him his money.

However, despite signing a court agreement, the agent only sent KSh 370,000.

In his words:

"I was expecting the four remaining games on Sunday. When I took the tickets to the betting shop, they told me the winnings have been withdrawn and I protested."

When he questioned why they had not paid him fully, they told him to find out who had withdrawn the money.

Lukman added that he had not received any other money since then and had evidence to prove it.

Reactions as man calls out betting platform

Nandi man allegedly loses KSh 1.2m from the 1.5m he won

In an almost similar case, Collins Kiptoo from Nandi slept a millionaire and woke up poor.

This was after the KSh 1.2 million he had left in his account after winning a bet disappeared overnight.

Out of the KSh 1.5 million he won, he could only withdraw KSh 300,000 before the rest went missing.

When he inquired from the betting offices, he was allegedly informed that auto-bets had eaten it up.

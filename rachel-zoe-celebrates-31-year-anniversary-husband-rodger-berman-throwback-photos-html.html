Rachel Zoe celebrates her 31 year anniversary with husbandRodger Berman with some throwback photos

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Rachel Zoe celebrated her 31 year anniversary with her husband Rodger Berman with a flurry of fun throwback photos.

Taking to Instagram on Monday, the stylist, 50, shared a video slideshow of images from her years with the banker, 53, and penned a heartfelt caption. 

The couple began dating while attending university in Washington, D.C. in 1991, they married in 1996.

'I have loved you @rbermanus from this day 8.29 ❤️ 31 years ago… we have done this life together and I cannot wait to see what is yet to come. Happiest anniversary to my person ❤️ And Yes everyone we met in kindergarten,' she penned.

Recalling the time they first met, Rodger previously said that he thought Rachel was the most beautiful woman he had ever seen.   

'I met Rachel in the summer of 1991. She was 19, and I was 22. She was a restaurant hostess, and I was working as a waiter in Washington, D.C., where we both attended college,' he told Harper's Bazaar in 2011. 

'I vividly remember the first time I saw her, wearing a tight black minidress, high-heeled pumps, stick-straight hair, and big red lips covered in M.A.C. Chili lipstick (her trademark).

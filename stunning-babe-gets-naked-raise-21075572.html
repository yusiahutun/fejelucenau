Stunning babe gets naked to raise 1,400 for UK homeless charity this Xmas

Publish date: 2024-03-28

A stunning Instagram model has spread Christmas cheer by stripping off for a homelessness charity.

Gemma McCourt put her 62K followers on Twitter and 16.2K on Instagram to good use by declaring a 24-hour charity fundraiser.

The 22-year-old announced the fundraiser alongside saucy content from her OnlyFans account - a subscription content service that allows creators to provide exclusive content to subscribers.

She promised any penny spent on the account in a 24-hour period would directly go to UK-based charity Crisis for its work helping needy people this Christmas.

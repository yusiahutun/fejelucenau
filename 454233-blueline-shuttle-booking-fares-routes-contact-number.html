Blueline Shuttle booking, fares, routes, and contact number

Publish date: 2024-04-26

Blueline shuttle is one of the fast transportation companies that serve various routes in Western parts of Kenya. It is a reliable transport service between Nairobi to Kakamega through Kapsabet town. If you are looking forward to travelling fast and by flexible means, then you should consider using their services.

Blueline Shuttle is popular for transporting passengers from Nairobi to Kakamega and a few towns and cities in Kenya. Since its launch, the company has been offering quite affordable prices in the transport industry.

Blueline Shuttle background

Blueline is one of the shuttles from Nairobi to Kakamega through the Kapsabet route. The company has been in existence for more than 10 years. It started with 11-seater vehicles and has gradually been adopting buses.

The transport company offers superb transport and parcel services between Nairobi and Western parts of Kenya. Anyone travelling from Nairobi to Kakamega and Mumias via Kapsabet, Shamakhokho, or Chavakali can use these vehicles.

Blueline Shuttle routes

The company operates mainly in the Nothern and Western parts of Kenya. You can use the Blueline Safaris on the following routes:

  • Nairobi to Kapsabet.
  • Nairobi to Kakamega.
  • Nairobi to Mumias.
  • Nairobi to Luanda.
  • Nairobi to Malava.
  • Malava to Nairobi.
  • Luanda to Nairobi.
  • Mumias to Nairobi.
  • Kakamega to Nairobi.
  • Kapsabet to Nairobi.

The offices are open for the following hours every day.

  • Monday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Tuesday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Wednesday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Thursday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Friday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Saturday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.
  • Sunday: 04:00 Hours - 20:00 Hours.

You can visit their offices within these hours per day for booking or any inquiries.

Blueline Shuttle online booking system

Blueline Shuttle doesn’t have an online booking portal because the company is yet to develop its website. However, if you want to reserve a seat for yourself, you can call their offices through their contact.

What is Blueline Shuttle fare?

Blueline Shuttle fare may vary from time to time depending on various factors. For instance, if you use these shuttles to Kakamega from Nairobi, you will pay from Ksh. 1,000 to Ksh. 1,400 during normal days. However, when the traffic of passengers is high, the fare rises.

Blueline Shuttle parcel services

Apart from transporting passengers, the shuttle offers parcel and delivery services around the routes plied by the shuttle services. The charges for the parcel services are quite pocket friendly and affordable.

What are the Blueline Shuttle Kenya contacts and addresses?

The main office is located in Mfangano Trade Centre along Mfangano Street. Blueline Shuttle contacts are as follows:

  • Physical Address: Mfangano Trade Centre along Mfangano Street.
  • Telephone number 1: +254 716 791 633.
  • Telephone number 2: +254 718 791 633.

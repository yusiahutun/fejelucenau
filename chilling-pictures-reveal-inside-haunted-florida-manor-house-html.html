Chilling pictures reveal inside a 'haunted' Florida manor house

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Eerie images reveal the inside of a 'haunted' manor house where two girls were once kidnapped and where a decapitated head was also buried in the garden.

The photos show the Dr Horace Drew Manor in Jacksonville, Florida, a vintage car left forgotten in the garage and a piano that remains in the living room alongside a sofa and extremely old television.  

In the 1970s, two girls were kidnapped and raped after a group of eleven students from Ribault High School broke into the decrepit mansion on the evening of Friday the 13th. 

According to a police report from the time, two men were said to have 'barged in, one armed with a sawed-off shotgun and the other with a knife'.

After demanding 'reefers' from the terrified students, then insisting the kids put their money into a hat they passed around, 'they herded the others into a bathroom, took the keys to a car from one of the boys, and drove off with two of the girls'.

One of the large reception rooms at the Dr Horace Drew Manor in Jacksonville, Florida which is stuffed with broken furniture and mattresses 

The eerie house has been occupied several times over the past few decades but was recently left to fall into disrepair in the last ten years

Sofas and TVs litter the once grand reception rooms which still feature art deco curtain fittings and hardwood floors throughout

The kidnappers pulled the girls' 'sweaters and blouses over their heads,' so they couldn't see where they were going, and drove them to another abandoned house, where they reportedly raped them repeatedly.  

Two other children also discovered a head in the garden of the mansion after one of them fell into a small hole. A Tampa Tribune headline from the time read, 'Human Head Found at 'Haunted House'.  

'Unbeknownst to most people at the time, there was also a human head buried in the backyard of the mansion around this time,' he said.

In the garage there is an old car left abandoned and several boxes filled with building materials among the crumbling stones of the outbuilding

Upstairs the attic is filled with trinkets and mementos collected by scores of previous owners including a pool table, old records and files

Toys and christmas decorations litter the floor of one of the upstairs rooms as well as piles of jewelry collected by past owners

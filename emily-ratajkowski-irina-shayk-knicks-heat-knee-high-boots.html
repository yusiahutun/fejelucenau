Emily Ratajkowski and Irina Shayk Enjoy Girls Night at Knicks Game

Publish date: 2024-03-25

The New York Knicks took on the Miami Heat on Friday night in what was an incredibly close match. New York ultimately won by two, with a score of 100-98, but the nail-biting game wasn’t the only thing that captured the attention of Madison Square Garden’s stands—model pals Irina Shayk and Emily Ratajkowski sat courtside, putting on a highly fashionable display.

Michelle Farsi/ NY Knicks/ MSG Sports

The My Body author basically dispelled the “Don’t mix black and brown” rule, cutting a stylish figure in a brown unbuttoned cardigan over a matching bralette, loose-fitting black trousers, and a long black leather coat. She cinched her mid-rise pants at the waist with a wide black belt that featured a gold buckle and paired the outfit with black pointed-toe boots that featured a gold stiletto heel. She accessorized with a small black purse and gold jewelry, including mini hoops, a choker, and a bangle. For glam, EmRata wore black winged eyeliner and a brown lip, and styled her hair down and parted in the middle.

Shayk transitioned a summer dress into a winter outfit, donning a black puffer coat over a plunging white silk frock that featured a crisscross neckline and black lace detailing along the hem. She paired the look with this season’s fall It girl shoes—black knee-high heeled boots. The supermodel rounded things off with large silver hoops and a matte red lip.

Shayk attended another Knicks game earlier this month sporting another pair of fabulous boots, this time thigh-high and leopard printed. She let her footwear make a statement, opting for a black turtleneck top as a minidress, a gold watch, and natural makeup.

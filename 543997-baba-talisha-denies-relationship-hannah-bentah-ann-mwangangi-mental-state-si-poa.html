Baba Talisha Denies Any Relationship with Hannah Bentah and Ann Mwangangi: &quot;My Mental State Si Poa&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-27
  • Baba Talisha has come out to declare his relationship status after online speculations about who he was with
  • He had been linked to TikToker Hannah Bentah and morgue attendant Ann Mwangangi, but he revealed that he was single.
  • Baba T also disclosed that he was not doing okay mentally as he cautioned those insulting him on social media

TikToker Baba Talisha has broken silence about his relationship status, a day after netizens speculated that he was in a relationship with mortician Ann Mwangangi.

Baba T and Ann had recently stepped out rocking matching outfits that sparked speculations on whether they were in a relationship.

Hannah Bentah questions Baba Talisha

However, the widowed father of one captioned his follow-up post insinuating they were just friends, but netizens did not believe him.

He was also recently put on the spot by his on-and-off girlfriend, Hannah Bentah, who questioned where she stood in his life.

But Baba T failed to answer her as he went around the question while Hannah probed about their relationship.

Why Baba T is not dating women linked to him

However, the father of one has changed his tune and said he is not in a relationship with anyone and maintained his mental state was not okay.

"Women's fights on TikTok or any other space that is on them. I have a life, and I have a kid to look after. I have my own life that I am trying to figure out. I am trying to help other people. My mental health state is not okay at the moment. Personally, I am not okay and you are all there insulting me," he said.

He added that he finds himself explaining things anytime he creates content with any woman, as people assume they are together.

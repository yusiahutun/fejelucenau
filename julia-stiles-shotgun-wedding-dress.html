Julia Stiles just had a &quot;shotgun&quot; wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Julia Stiles and Preston J. Cook have married in what Stiles has jokingly referred to as a "shotgun" wedding.

The Riviera actress and her camera assistant partner - who are expecting their first child together - had an intimate ceremony with two close friends on the beach in Seattle on Labour Day, according to her representative, reports E!.

The 36-year-old star shared a picture of both herself and Cook placing their hands on her baby bump - wedding rings visible - on her Instagram account.

She added the caption: "Who doesn't love a shotgun wedding?"

Stiles' low-key white lace wedding dress is from Tiffany Rose Maternity and costs £199.

The 10 Things I Hate About You star got engaged to Cook on Christmas Eve at a private residence in Isla Grande in Colombia, telling People earlier this year: "That's where we got engaged.

"All around, it was a really wonderful trip. We stayed on an island that was really beautiful and there was a lot of great snorkelling. The city was really nice, too, but because I live in the city the natural scenery was really great."

She added that she had been taking a relaxed approach to tying the knot, adding: "I'm not getting a ton of marital advice.

"We're really slow on the wedding planning so I haven't really been soliciting advice. Somebody told me about radical empathy, but I'm still trying to figure out what that means. I'm trying to figure out what makes it radical."

Stiles confirmed her pregnancy earlier this year, and "couldn't resist" debuting her baby bump with a sweet selfie on her Instagram last month.

The happy couple met on the set of Blackway in 2014.

Madison Beer Announces European Tour

Publish date: 2024-03-19

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) — Singer-songwriter Madison Beer announced plans for a tour of Europe in early 2024.

Beer’s ‘Spinnin Tour’ kicks off at Stockholm’s Fryshuset Arena on February 24th and concludes at the Eventim Apollo in London on March 25th.

Along the way, Beer will perform in key markets such as Paris, Barcelona, Brussels, and Milan, among others.

The tour’s presales will begin on Tuesday 10 October at 12pm, local time with general onsale beginning on Friday 10 October at 10am, local time at madisonbeer.com.

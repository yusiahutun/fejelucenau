Hottest and Coldest Temperatures Ever in Boston on April 26

Publish date: 2024-07-08

On April 26th in Boston, the weather varies from the hottest temperature ever recorded at 87.1°F in 2009 to the coldest temperature recorded at 35.1°F in 1972. The average temperature for this date is 50.09°F, with an average high of 54.97°F and an average low of 45.21°F. In 2009, an interesting news item to note is that this was the year when the swine flu pandemic, also known as H1N1, spread across the world, affecting many countries including the United States. It caused a significant public health concern and led to various health measures being implemented to control its spread.

Alexa Chung x AG Jeans Fall Collection Has Landed

Publish date: 2024-04-22

British model, author, designer, muse, and perpetual It-girl Alexa Chung has one of those dreamy wardrobes that pretty much every single woman in the world envies–she even has a Mulberry bag named in her honor and picked up the award for British Style Icon not one, but three years in a row.

If you fall into the category of fashion girl who’s dying to dress like Alexa, listen up: Chung’s second and final collaboration with AG Jeans has just landed, and it’s a goodie.

The fall collection is just starting to drop online and in stores, and comprises mostly various denim silhouettes , T-shirts, and slouchy sweaters, and—phew—a version of last season’s buzzy A-line button-down mini skirt that became an Instagram star in its own right.

The range also includes some cool new graphic pieces, like the feminist tee we’re betting will be the first piece to sell out (and the piece to show up on all the street style blogs come September Fashion Month).

Although the line is on the expensive side (that feminist tee is $118, denim rompers clock in $345, and there are few suede pieces that hover between $800 and $1,200), we have no doubt Alexa’s legion of fans–including the 1.8 million followers she has on Instagram–will cough it up to inch closer to emulating her signature style.

Kety Lonestar Skirt, $178; at AG Jeans

Travis Kelce spotted filming 'Are You Smarter Than a Celebrity?' as fans say he was 'made for TV'

Publish date: 2024-09-04

Travis Kelce is currently taking the next steps in his career, stepping into his hosting duties as he’s currently filming ‘Are You Smarter Than a Celebrity?’ and BTS pics have dropped of him looking dapper.

The NFL star is a man of many talents, from winning the Super Bowl and hosting TV shows, to winning over Taylor Swift, is there anything this man can’t do?

Travis Kelce looks dapper while filming ‘Are You Smarter Than A Celebrity?’

Last month, rumors began to surface the 34-year-old was in talks to host a reboot of the hit TV show, Are You Smarter Than A Fifth Grader? Now, it’s been revealed the ‘reboot’ is actually Are You Smarter Than a Celebrity? and it looks like he’s filling his new role with ease.

Although we’re not sure when we’ll be able to see Kelce in full force on our TV screens just yet, luckily, Swifties managed to get their hands on some BTS pics which are now circulating on X, formerly known as Twitter.

In the snaps, the star can be seen all smiles, looking dapper in a blue suit over a white shirt, with a fresh trim standing next to quarterback Ryan Fitzpatrick.

“Man he was born to be in front of a camera,” wrote one fan.

“Look how he ate that!!!” exclaimed a second.

It’s not Kelce’s first time in a hosting role. In 2016, long before he was dating Swift, the NFL star hosted his own dating show, Catching Kelce in which girls would ‘compete’ for his heart. He’s also had a hosting gig on SNL, something he said was a ‘dream come true’.

Kelce and Swift spotted during LA date night

The celebrity couple have been spending some quality time together lately as they both take well-deserved breaks from their extremely busy careers.

Trav and Tay were spotted enjoying dinner at the Bird Street Club in West Hollywood on Tuesday, as they attempted to duck in the car away from paps.

In March, while the Bad Blood singer started a break from her Eras Tour and Kelce enjoyed some off-season time, they headed off to the Bahamas, as sources told Life & Style the Kansas City Chiefs star told his girlfriend he wanted to ‘spend the rest of his life with her’.

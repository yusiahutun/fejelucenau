Let’s start witht the trio of ingredients that tackle hyperpigmentation. Niacinamide has a laundry list of skin-enhancing powers, some of which include minimizing hyperpigmentation, controlling sebum production, boosting hydration, and improving the skin barrier function. Tranexamic acid is known to brighten, even skin tone, lessen redness, and fade dark spots. Lastly, arbutin boasts melanin synthesis inhibitory properties, meaning it can help lighten skin tone. When a product has this blend of dark spot-fighting ingredients, it’s no wonder why TikTokers and shoppers are hooked.

To back up these powerful elements, ceramides act as the glue that binds skin cells together. They maintain a healthy skin barrier and lock in moisture, while hyaluronic acid increases hydration by retaining water.

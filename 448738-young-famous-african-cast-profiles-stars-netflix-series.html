Have you watched Young, Famous, & African? If not, what are you waiting for? This reality television show brings together some of your favourite African celebrities. So, who are the Young, Famous, & African cast members?

Young, Famous, & African is a television series you cannot afford to miss. It follows a group of famed, affluent young media stars in Africa. They struggle to build their careers, look for love and rekindle old flames.

A group of wealthy and influential African men and women have teamed up to give you an entertaining show. So, here are the names of these celebrities.

Innocent Idibia is professionally known as 2Baba. He is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, record producer, actor, and entrepreneur. He is one of the most influential and wealthiest celebrities in Africa.

2Baba was born on September 18th, 1975, in Jos, Plateau. He began his music career while attending the Institute of Management & Technology (IMT) in Enugu. In 2015, he became the first winner of MTV Europe’s Best African Act Award.

Innocent Idibia is the husband of the co-actor, Annie Macaulay. They have been married since 2012 and share two children.

2. Zari Hassan

Zari, the self-proclaimed Boss Lady, is a Ugandan socialite, musician and businesswoman. She resides in South Africa, where she runs businesses. She is the CEO of Brooklyn City College, which she co-founded with her late husband, Ivan Semwanga.

Hassan was born on September 23rd, 1980, in Jinja, Uganda. She attended school in London from an early age. She returned to Uganda in 2000 and later moved to South Africa, where she met and married Ivan Semwanga.

Zari is a mother of five children. She had three children with her late husband and two with her ex-partner and now co-actor, Diamond Platinumz.

3. Jeremiah Ogbodo

Jeremiah Ogbodo is famously known as Swanky Jerry. He is a Nigerian celebrity fashion stylist. He has worked with famous African artists, including Davido, 2Baba, and D'Banji.

Ogbodo was born on July 4th, 1991, in Lagos, Nigeria. He is a graduate of the School of Style New York. His appearance on Young, Famous and African on Netflix has made him more famous.

Swanky began his career with the launch of his brand Swanky Signatures Styling in June 2012. He won the Fashion Stylist of the Year at the Lagos Fashion Awards in 2014. He is currently unmarried and has never had children.

4. Khanyisile Mbau

Khanyi Mbau is a renowned South African actress, singer, and media personality. She has appeared in many South African television productions. She is known for her distinctive acting technique.

Mbau was born on October 25th, 1985, in Johannesburg, South Africa. Before she became an actress, she did modelling. She rose to fame with roles in the television series Muvhango and mini-series After Nine.

Mbau is the self-proclaimed OG of the group who embodies the kind of wealth she looks for in the men she pursues. She tackles her golddigger reputation head-on and does not apologise for setting the bar high.

5. Nadia Nakai Kandava

Nadia is a South African-born Zimbabwean rapper, songwriter and television personality. She is famous for her song Naaa Mean which was released in 2017. She was nominated for Artist of the Decade at the 2021 South African Hip Hop Awards.

Kandava was born on May 18th, 1990, in South Africa. She was born to a South African father and a Zimbabwean mother. She is impressive on Young, Famous and African.

6. Quinton Paul Masina

Quinton is famously known as Naked. He is a South African radio personality and club DJ. He fancies a lavish lifestyle and enjoys it to the fullest.

Naked was born on August 12th, 1980, in Benoni Town, Gauteng, South Africa. He has had many relationships, making him a topic for internet gossip. He previously appeared in the Real Housewives of Johannesburg with his ex-wife, Naledi Willers.

Paul Masina is now dating footballer Kayleigh Schwark. Kayleigh also appears on Young, Famous and African. He is often the voice of harsh reason for friends like Diamond and Andile when it comes to quarrels involving multiple women.

7. Andile Ncube

Andile is a South African TV personality and producer. He has been in the television industry since 2003 and has won the hearts of many fans. He is best known for hosting the Mzansi Friday night music show.

8. Nasibu Abdul Juma Issack

Nasibu Abdul is professionally known as Diamond Platnumz. He is a renowned Tanzanian singer and dancer. He is the first sub-Saharan African artist to earn over 1 billion YouTube views and has received three BET Award nominations.

Diamond Platnumz was born on October 2nd, 1989, in Tandale, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. He came into the limelight after the release of his hit Mbagala. He is single and a father of four children.

9. Kayleigh Schwark

Kayleigh Schwark is a South African model, football star, and celebrity partner. She is also passionate about fitness. She plays for Luso Africa, a team competing in the Sasol women's league football division.

Kayleigh was born on December 4th, 1994, in Midrand, Gauteng, South Africa. She was raised alongside her sister, Tracey Lee Julius. She was catapulted to fame following her appearance on Young, Famous and African.

Schwark is trying to create a new life with Naked. She often looks to Nadia and Khanyi for advice about their relationship when she is frustrated. She currently lives with her boyfriend.

10. Annie Macaulay-Idibia

Annie Macaulay is a Nigerian model, singer, actress, and businesswoman. She is also the wife of singer Innocent Idibia, AKA 2Baba. She was nominated in the Best Supporting Actress category at 2009's Best of Nollywood Awards.

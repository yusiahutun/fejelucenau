GMA's Lara Spencer's luxury foyer in her Connecticut home looks like a five star hotel

Publish date: 2024-04-20

What a welcome!

    Lara Spencer's beautiful luxury home in Connecticut may give you a little interior decor envy - and her spacious and stunningly-designed foyer looks like it would fit in perfectly to any five star hotel!

    The star has shared a few photos of the gorgeous space, whilst giving fans a peek at her beloved dog, Riva, who likes to lie by the front door - often waiting for her son, Duff, to come home. Of course the pictures of the pooch are adorable, but there's no doubt that her incredible home stole the show.

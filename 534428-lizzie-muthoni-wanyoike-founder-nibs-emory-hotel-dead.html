Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike, Founder of NIBS and Emory Hotel Is Dead

Publish date: 2024-04-30
  • Kenyan businesswoman and entrepreneur Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike, founder of NIBS and Emory Hotel, died after a short illness
  • Wanyoike was a celebrated educator who received several awards and honours from the Presidency and Forbes
  • Wanyoike shared a message of faith and prayer on her Facebook page less than 12 hours before her death

Amos Khaemba, a journalist at TUKO.co.ke, brings over three years of experience covering politics and current affairs in Kenya.

Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike, a renowned entrepreneur and educator who founded the Nairobi Institute of Business Studies (NIBS) and the Emory Hotel, has died at age 72.

TUKO.co.ke has learned that Wanyoike died on Sunday after an illness.

What Lizzie Wanyoike's family said

Wanyoike was a visionary leader who transformed Kenya's education and hospitality sector. She started NIBS and grew it to become one of the leading private colleges in the country, offering courses in business, technology, journalism, hospitality, and beauty.

“In loving memory of a remarkable educator. Her passion ignited minds, her kindness touched hearts. A beacon of wisdom, she left an indelible mark on countless lives, shaping futures with grace,” a message from her family read.

She also established the Emory Hotel in 2016, a four-star facility.

Wanyoike was a recipient of several awards and honours, including the Order of the Grand Warrior of Kenya (OGW), the Moran of the Order of the Burning Spear (MBS), and the Head of State Commendation and Forbes Magazine also recognised her as a Top 100 Woman Entrepreneur in 2012.

Wanyoike was a devout Christian who shared her faith and inspiration with others. Before her death, she posted a message on her Facebook page, quoting St. Mark 11:24:

“Therefore I tell you, whatever you ask for in prayer, believe that you have received it, and it will be yours. May God answer all you have prayed for. Have a wonderful Sunday.”

How Kenyans mourned Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike

Her death has shocked and saddened those who knew her or benefited from her work. Among those who have mourned her are:

Sammy Gitahi wrote on Facebook:

“A phenomenal lady has left; she has left too soon. Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike wa NIBS technical college R.I.P”

Sonniah De’Njambie wrote:

“REST IN PEACE. Devastating news following the death of influencial businesslady who is also the C.E.O and founder of NIBS Technical College and Emory hotel Madam Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike”

Polycarp Hinga wrote on X:

"Nairobi Institute of Business Studies (NIBS) College principal and owner of Emory Hotel Mama Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike just died!”

Government Spokesperson Isaac Mwaura wrote on X:

"My most sincere condolences to the family and friends of Lizzie Muthoni Wanyoike of NIBS College. An educationist and mentor to many, may she rest in eternal peace."

Her funeral arrangements will be announced later. May her soul rest in peace.

Gigi Hadid Layers a Corset Over a Bubblegum Pink Button-Down

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Gigi Hadid displayed a unique layering trick while celebrating the launch of her new knitwear line, Guest in Residence.

The supermodel stepped out in New York City last night wearing a form-fitting champagne corset layered over a bubblegum pink button-down, which was left unbuttoned. She paired the statement piece with a pair of ivory straight-leg trousers with raw hems. She matched the rest of her outfit with her pink top, including her pointed-toe pumps and her mini leather Miu Miu bag. For accessories, she wore multiple gold necklaces and a few gold earring cuffs.

Getty Images

Getty Images

Gigi's friends and family also showed up to support her knitwear launch.

Bella Hadid arrived hand in hand with her boyfriend, Marc Kalman, in coordinating all-black outfits.

Wearing a black cardigan over a cropped white T-shirt, Bella paired the look with high-waisted midi-length black leggings, two black belts, white crew-length socks, and black pumps. She accessorized with black shades, a silver medallion choker necklace, and a black purse with silver embellishments.

Kalman wore a black knit sweater over a white tank top, black baggy trousers, and a pair of black Doc Martens.

Gotham//Getty Images

Meanwhile, Emily Ratajkowski opted for a masculine look in a cropped pinstriped blue button-down from Miu Miu and a pair of wide-leg navy blue trousers. She finished the look with tan sandals and the same Miu Miu bag that Gigi carried, though Ratajkowski's was in black.

Gotham//Getty Images

Gigi announced the upcoming launch of Guest in Residence in an Instagram post in early August, sharing a behind-the-scenes look at the brand's making.

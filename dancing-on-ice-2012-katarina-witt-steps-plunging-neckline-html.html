And she should have received her very own special award for making such an astonishing entrance.

Stepping on to the red carpet, Katarina donned a lavish gold sequined gown with black belt and high heeled shoes.

But even the large shoulder pads couldn't detract from her cleavage.

And she appeared to be enjoying the attention as she happily posed to emphasise her figure while she grinned at the cameras.

Drawing attention: The figure skater dressed to impress in a gold figure-hugging frock

Even her jewellery drew attention to her breasts, carefully placing a necklace around her neck.

She's become known for showing more than her talent on the celebrity skating show, contending with former host Holly Willoughby, who became notorious for her low-cut outfits on the family programme.

But Katarina looks set to continue making an impact on viewers with her outfit choices.

Lapping it up: The German beauty has been enjoying the attention her assets have brought

She made a name for herself when she showed off her figure in Playboy magazine in 1998.

And even though it has been over 10 years since Katarina Witt's now infamous spread, the 46-year-old figure skater is still a fan of displaying her curves to their best potential.

The German beauty stunned viewers with an extremely low-cut dress on tonight's Dancing On Ice, causing many of them to take to Twitter and comment on her cleavage.

Stealing the show: Katarina has been pushing the boat out with her cleavage-baring ensembles

One viewer tweeted: 'Katarina Witt's cleavage takes over my screen on Sundays. Really that necessary? We know U look hot but put it away now and then!'

The original: Holly Willoughby on the show in 2008

And another added: 'Katarina Witt is getting progressively more daring with the amount of cleavage she is showing.'

Katarina appears to have taken over from former Dancing On Ice presenter Holly Willoughby.

Holly previously caused controversy with her array of low-cut outfits, particularly the plunging cream gown she donned back in 2008.

But Holly's replacement Christine Bleakley said when she was announced as the new host of the show that she wouldn't be competing with Holly on the cleavage front.

She said: 'I think will ease my way in gently with my wardrobe.

'Long dresses suit the occasion and maybe a bit of sparkle, but no cleavage.

'I’m lacking in the Holly Willoughbooby department. I’m happy to say that. I’ll keep it simple!'

However, Christine looked stunning in her outfit choice on tonight's show.

The 32-year-old presenter wowed in a Suzanne Neville dress and Jimmy Choo heels for the programme, with her hair teased into a high, scraped-back ponytail.

Meanwhile, actress Charlene Tilton, 53, became the latest contestant to be voted off Dancing On Ice following a performance to the hit song Poker Face by Lady Gaga.

However, the Dallas star didn't seem to upset about her departure and instead praised everyone for the 'amazing experience' she'd had.

'I feel alright,' she told host Philip Schofield after the judging panel decided to save TV presenter Sam Nixon.

'I have worked with the best wardrobe and make-up team I have ever worked with, I've had fun.'

She then went on to tell judge Louie Spence that she wants him to come to Los Angeles.

'You're going to be my new BFF,' she shouted to him as she grabbed the microphone off of Philip.

Taylor Swift Wore a Fluffy $1,400 Red Coat, but I Found a $38 Lookalike

Publish date: 2024-07-27

I’m grateful that Taylor Swift found Travis Kelce for more reasons than one. Yes, I want my girl to be happy, and, of course, I’m here for the power couple vibes — but can we talk about her Chiefs apparel? The pop singer has been cheering her beau on all season long, wearing themed jackets, on-brand shoes, and snuggly team sweatshirts. What can I say? She simply loves the color red, whether she’s styling it as a scarf, wearing it as statement lipstick, or naming her album after the shade. And she kept her red style streak going by reaching for a festive coat for Sunday’s Chiefs game. 

Alongside Lyndsay Bell and Brittany Mahomes, Swift wore a Stella McCartney belted teddy coat (which retails at about $1,400 and has since sold out), and paired it with a Louis Vouitton mini skirt. She accessorized with a pair of Vrai earrings and a Cartier necklace (because, duh), and completed the look with a pair of soaring tall Stuart Weitzman over-the-knee boots (which are also practically sold out on Nordstrom, Saks Off 5th, and Zappos). But with the most affordable item on Swift’s list ringing in at $950, it’s a bit difficult to recreate her exact look. Thankfully, you can secure some spot-on red coat lookalikes, starting at just $38. Not only would they make for a great fashion statement to a Chiefs football game à la Swift, but they’ll work for all of your holiday plans, too. 

Levi’s Quilted Teddy Coat

This Levi’s teddy coat captures the overall essence of Swift’s outer layer. It doesn't only include the same fuzzy material, but it extends towards the knees and boasts an oversized collar. Hidden beneath the snap button closure, you’ll find a center zipper to ensure your coat stays closed and secure no matter what you’re up to. Even better, you can look forward to two lower pockets to keep your hands warm all winter long.

Prettygarden Faux Shearling Oversized Jacket

For a more casual take on Swift’s statement coat, consider this best-selling Prettygarden jacket, which offers a cropped version of the look. The boxy, oversized fit is spacious and comfortable, while cinched sleeves, a lapel collar, and zipper front provide just the right amount of structure. Dress it down with jeans and street sneakers, or amp it up with a leather mini skirt, sheer tights, and moto boots.

Bernardo Double-Breasted Coat

If you like the OG coat’s striking red hue but aren’t sure about the fluffy material, consider this sleek Bernardo double-breasted coat. On sale for $130 right now — which is 40 percent off its original price tag, BTW — enjoy a tapered silhouette, back vent, four stylized buttons, and two pockets. The back center seam also adds a touch of elegance, allowing this coat to be worn just about anywhere. It can easily be styled with a pair of nude pumps for a museum walk, or with some rain boots for a casual coffee run.

Hairy OnlyFans teacher sacked after leaked 'naughty at work' pic was taken in school loo

Publish date: 2024-06-27

An OnlyFans teacher who allegedly made racy content in the school where she worked has been given the boot.

Preschool teacher Brenna Percy, who taught at Wolf Swamp Road School in Massachusetts, US, moonlighted as an adult content creator and posted a saucy photo from the school's bathroom on November 3, 2021, captioned: "naughty at work".

Percy, who went by the name "hairyboo" on both OnlyFans and a similar website, Fansly, was caught out when the snap was leaked to far-right Twitter account Libs of TikTok, who promptly shared it with the school.

On the afternoon of January 24, the school superintendent called members of the Twitter page and said: "She [Brenna Percy] no longer works for Longmeadow Public Schools."

The school’s website also removed Percy from its staff section in the wake of the NSFW revelation.

It isn't clear yet whether Percy will be banned from educating entirely, as previous teachers have been in similar situations including middle school science teacher Samantha Peer who was fired after her students found a series of OnlyFans videos and snaps that appeared to have been filmed in the classroom.

However, her OnlyFans account - where she has posted more than 5,000 pictures and over 300 videos racking up close to 100k likes - has remained active since the incident.

The Twitter page Libs of TikTok was started up in November 2020 and has amassed a sizeable cult fanbase of around 1.8m followers since then, which includes conservative groups and right-wing politicians.

The account is known for its anti-LGBTQ+ stance and also regularly takes aim at people with left-wing politics as well as downplaying the severity of the Covid pandemic.

The viral page also showed support for the election fraud conspiracy theory spearheaded by Donald Trump during the 2020 US presidential elections.

A Historic Train Station

Did you know that Manchester Central Convention Complex was once a fully operational train station? Originally known as Manchester Central Railway Station, it first opened its doors to the public in 1880.

A Symbol of Architectural Grandeur

The complex is an architectural marvel, showcasing exquisite Victorian design. Its beautiful arched entrance, stunning glass roof, and elegant details make it a standout attraction in Manchester.

The Largest Conference Venue in the North of England

With over 23,000 square meters of event space, Manchester Central Convention Complex is the largest conference venue in the North of England. It can accommodate events of all sizes, from small meetings to large-scale conferences.

A Green Venue

Manchester Central is committed to sustainability and environmental responsibility. The venue has implemented initiatives to reduce carbon footprint and has received recognition for its eco-friendly practices.

A Hub for Creative Events

The complex has become a go-to destination for creative events such as art exhibitions, design showcases, and fashion shows. Its versatile spaces offer a perfect backdrop for artistic expression.

Home to the Manchester International Festival

One of the most celebrated cultural events in the city, the Manchester International Festival, takes place at Manchester Central. This festival showcases a diverse range of artistic performances and installations.

A Star-Studded Venue

FAQs

1. What is the history behind the Manchester Central Convention Complex?

The Manchester Central Convention Complex, originally known as the Manchester Central Railway Station, opened its doors in 1880. It served as a bustling railway station until its closure in 1969. It was later transformed into a convention center, reopening in 1986.

2. How many event spaces does the Manchester Central Convention Complex have?

The complex boasts a total of 23 versatile event spaces, ranging from small meeting rooms to large exhibition halls. These spaces can accommodate various types of events, including conferences, exhibitions, and performances.

3. Can I visit the Manchester Central Convention Complex even if I’m not attending an event?

Yes, you can visit the complex even if you’re not attending a specific event. It is open to the public, and you can explore its stunning architecture, enjoy its on-site eateries and cafes, or simply soak in the vibrant atmosphere of this historic landmark.

4. Are there any nearby accommodations for visitors attending events at the Manchester Central Convention Complex?

Yes, there are several hotels located within walking distance of the complex. These accommodations cater to a range of budgets and preferences, ensuring that visitors have convenient access to the venue.

5. Can I host my own event at the Manchester Central Convention Complex?

Absolutely! The Manchester Central Convention Complex offers state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated events team to assist you in hosting your own event. Whether it’s a conference, exhibition, or private function, the complex provides the perfect setting for a successful event.

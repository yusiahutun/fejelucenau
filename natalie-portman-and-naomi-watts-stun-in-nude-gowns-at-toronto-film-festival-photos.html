Natalie Portman and Naomi Watts Stun in Nude Gowns at Toronto Film Festival!

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Nude has never looked better!

Natalie Portman hit the red carpet for the premiere of "A Tale Of Love And Darkness" during the 2015 Toronto Film Festival on Thursday in Toronto, Canada.

Portman stunned at the screening wearing a nude lace one-shoulder Lanvin custom gown. The former child star's dress also featured a gorgeous star-shaped brooch.

The "Black Swan" actress finished her look with bronze shadow, a bold pink lip and wore her hair styled in a slicked-back pony.

While we've never seen Natalie look better, she wasn't the only star rocking a nude design at the festival.

Naomi Watts likewise stole the spotlight at the premiere of her new movie, "Demolition" on Thursday in Toronto.

Watt's glowed on the red carpet wearing a ruffle 2016 Balmain Resort gown. The actress added a pop of color with a gold and black belt and finished her look with matching gold cuffs, nude sandals, and gold earrings. Watts styled her hair in a messy updo and sported a dark plum lip.

While we're loving both dresses, we gotta give the nod to Natalie -- she looks timeless in this custom Lanvin design.

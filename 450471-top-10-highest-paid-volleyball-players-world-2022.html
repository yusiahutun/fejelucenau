Top 10 highest-paid volleyball players in the world in 2022

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Volleyball may not be as big a sport like soccer, but it still is famous and has produced several global celebrities. To most, it is a sport played at the beach as a leisure activity, but professional players will tell you the opposite. Yes, it is a fun activity for pros and amateurs alike, but there is some serious money to be made from this game. A look at the highest-paid volleyball players will reveal just how big this sport has grown to be.

Volleyball courts are not as big as football stadiums, but that is just because only a small space is required for the play area; hence it isn't a reflection of how big or small the sport is. Furthermore, professional volleyball players are dedicated athletes who have sacrificed a lot to entertain the rest through this sport. Therefore, it is befitting for them to earn as much money as the best athletes in other sports.

Highest-paid volleyball players

It is a story of hierarchy as much as it is a life story. No two people will be equal in talent, so professional volleyball players' salaries will never be the same. According to World of Valley, some of the highest-paid players include:

1. Wilfredo Leon - $1.4 million

  • Full name: Wilfredo León Venero
  • Date of birth: 31st July 1993
  • Age: years 28 years (as of 2022)
  • Position: Outside hitter
  • Current team: Sir Safety Perugia

Cuban-Polish Wilfredo Leon ranks first among the highest-paid male volleyball players in the world. He plays for Sir Safety Perugia as an outside hitter. Leon is considered one of the most accomplished volleyball players ever.

By the age of 24, he had already won all the titles in professional volleyball. Wilfredo Leon’s salary is more than $1 million, and he is also among the richest volleyball players.

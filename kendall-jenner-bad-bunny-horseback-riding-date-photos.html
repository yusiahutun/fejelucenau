Kendall Jenner Took Bad Bunny on a Romantic Horseback Riding Date

Publish date: 2024-02-22

Kendall Jenner reentered her horse girl era to treat Bad Bunny to a romantic date.

In photos originally published by TMZ, the rumored couple was spotted horseback riding at the Hidden Hills Equestrian Center in California.

Some pictures capture the two sitting close together and riding the same horse, with the model even extending her arm to seemingly take a selfie with the rapper. In other snaps, Jenner steps off the horse to observe Bad Bunny riding solo around the corral.

For the date, she dressed casually, opting for a Western-themed off-duty look in a cropped white tank top, baggy jeans, a white baseball cap, a green flannel shirt tied around her waist, and brown boots. Bad Bunny looked equally cool in a cream jacket, brown trousers, white Adidas sneakers, and black sunglasses.

Dating rumors between the model and rapper first emerged when an anonymous tipster told celebrity gossip Instagram account DeuxMoi that they saw the two kissing at a club. Soon after, the two were seen together on a double date with Jenner's close friends, Hailey and Justin Bieber. The rumors only cemented when paparazzi snapped pics of Jenner and Bad Bunny exchanging a kiss last month.

An insider told People that the stars "are spending time together."

"Kendall recently started hanging out with him. They were introduced by friends. He moved to L.A. a few weeks ago and bought a house," another source said. "She likes him and is having fun. He is different from guys that she dated in the past. He is very charming."

Jenner previously dated NBA player Devin Booker for two years; they ultimately broke up in October 2022. Meanwhile, Bad Bunny previously dated jewelry designer Gabriela Berlingeri. He talked about meeting her in a 2020 interview with Rolling Stone.

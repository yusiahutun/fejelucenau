Jose Eber Hair Infrared Dryer

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Media Platforms Design Team

The last thing I have in mind in the summer is a 30-minute blow out on high heat, but a little surf spray doesn't always suffice. I like the new Jose Eber Infrared Blow Dryer, which uses the light in its name to cut down on drying time — it feels super powerful but doesn't overheat. Consider it your good summer hair compromise.

José Eber Hair Infrared Blow Dryer, $149, ulta.com.

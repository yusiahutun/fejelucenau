Prayers to Saint Anthony for lost items to be recovered

Publish date: 2024-02-24

Have you ever lost a precious gadget or item? If you have, for sure, you spent sleepless nights wondering where your item was or who might have stolen it. It's natural to be upset. You can find solace in the prayers to Saint Anthony for lost items.

St. Anthony of Padua is famous as the patron saint of lost things. He was a member of the religious order founded by St. Francis of Assisi. He was a great preacher and theological instructor. There are many prayers made to St. Anthony asking for his aid. Many prayers mention specific favours and requests, such as lost items and aid for travellers and pregnant women.

Why do people pray to Saint Anthony for lost items?

St. Anthony of Padua is the saint of finding lost things. The reason why St. Anthony is so often called upon for help in finding lost or stolen items can be traced back to an incident that took place during his stay at the Franciscan monastery at Montpellier.

He lost his book of psalms which was of tremendous value to him. A novice stole it. He went down on his knees and prayed to God to aid him in recovering the book. The prayer was answered as the novice returned the book back to him and asked for forgiveness.

Praying to St Anthony for the recovery of lost items is a Catholic tradition. Over the years, people have always turned to St. Anthony in prayer for lost items to be recovered. There is this belief that God answers the petition when a person prays to the saint with an open and honest heart.

St. Anthony, perfect imitator of Jesus, who received from God the special power of restoring lost things, grant that I may find [name the item] which has been lost. At least restore to me peace and tranquillity of mind, the loss of which has afflicted me even more than my material loss. To this favour, I ask another of you: that I may always remain in possession of the true good that is God. Let me rather lose all things than lose God, my supreme good. Let me never suffer the loss of my greatest treasure, eternal life with God. Amen

Prayer to experience the help and consolation of St Anthony

Here is another prayer of confidence you can make to St. Anthony to help you recover the lost item so that you can experience his help and consolation.

Blessed Saint Anthony, how great and mighty is the grace and power of God in thee; how many persons hast thou lovingly assisted, especially when they implored thy aid to recover lost (or stolen) things? Behold, I, too, humbly come to thee in my necessity; let me, though unworthy as I am, I beseech thee, experience thy help and consolation.I know the Infant Jesus, whom thou bearest in thy arms, will not refuse thy prayers. I, therefore, recommend to thee the object of my prayer that what was lost (or stolen) may, through thy intercession, be restored to me and that I may always rejoice in thy protection and love. Amen.

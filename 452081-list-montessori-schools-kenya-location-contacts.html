Publish date: 2024-05-11

If you are looking for a school environment that offers individualised, student-led learning, you have probably considered enrolling your child in a Montessori school. Montessori schools have many loyal devotees, and they're indeed rising in popularity. The list of Montessori schools in Kenya is a testimony that its unique pedagogies have found their way into Kenyan classrooms.

Montessori schools started from humble beginnings, with Dr. Maria's quest for quality education for young children. The Montessori Method of education is a child-centred educational approach that focuses on the development of the whole child holistically in all spheres, namely physical, intellectual, linguistic, emotional and social.

What is the Montessori method of teaching?

The Montessori method of education combines a philosophy of freedom and self-development for children with a practical approach. As a result, children are given the opportunity to develop essential life skills that allow them greater freedom in the classroom.

What are the five principles of the Montessori method?

This approach emphasises educating the whole child, covering all aspects of development, namely intellectual, social, physical, emotional and spiritual.

What are the disadvantages of Montessori education?

Montessori education has got its own drawbacks. The schools are expensive, and only privileged children can afford to enrol in them. Besides, the open-ended structure of the classroom can be intimidating to some students.

How much is Montessori Schools fees?

The school fees vary across the different schools. Once you have identified the ideal Montessori school for your child, you can inquire about fees using the contact details provided.

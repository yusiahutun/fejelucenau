Amber Hargrove is an American actress as well as an Army personnel who shot to fame after got featured in the popular television show ‘Naked and Afraid’. She has been considered an amazing survival expert and her time at the US Army provided her all the reliable tools to cope with the Discovery Reality Shows. Amy is among the strongest and the most efficient women ever known in the show. She risks her life every day and had to do hardships, to make her country proud.

Early Life

Amber Hargrove was born in Oroville, California on 26th October 1986. She is a daughter to proud parents James and Cathy Betti. Her family supported her when she was at the lowest and motivated her to make correct life choices. She donates her free time to camping, hiking, and fishing with her parents. However, as per the records, she has completed her High School education and went to college for her further studies.

Amber Hargrove Career

While Amber was in school, her batch mates used to consider her as an extremely competitive lady as she used to participate in different events and would always give her best to win and never give up easily. Prior to catching all the media’s attention, she served for the US Army and was the strongest of all for 11 years long. Her journey being an Army wasn’t easy.

She has to deal with struggles and have to risk her life. While getting trained, she learned about mental and physical toughness, which helped her to complete her next journey on television. She signed up for the Discovery Reality Channel in 2015, the show is all about surviving for 40 days with water, food, and clothes.

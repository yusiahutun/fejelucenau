Taylor Swift and Travis steal Super Bowl limelight but Jason Kelce won the after party

Publish date: 2024-07-16

At Sunday’s Super Bowl, everyone was focused on Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce, but really, they should’ve been focusing on his brother Jason.

We wish we could be as free as Jason Kelce, who was seen at the Super Bowl having the time of his life. The 36-year-old was spotted spending time with his brother Travis Kelce and his girlfriend after the Kansas City Chiefs win on Sunday (February 11). The Chiefs were playing against the San Francisco 49ers and ended up winning 25-22, even though it didn’t look like it was going in their favor; up until halftime, the 49ers were in the lead with 10-3.

Jason Kelce has the time of his life at the Super Bowl after party

Jason was joined by his and Travis’ mom and dad, Donna and Ed to watch the game. His wife Kylie also joined him in the stands to support her celebrity brother-in-law. It was a whole family affair as Taylor’s parents, Andrea and Scott, were also in attendance.

But when it came to the celebrations, Jason was the key star of the night.

On X (formerly Twitter) fans shared videos of the Philadelphia Eagles player following Travis and Taylor Swift around like a lost puppy at the Super Bowl after party.

People said he looked like an imaginary friend and compared it to Ryan Reynolds’ new film. In the movie, a young girl can see everyone’s imaginary friends and seeks to reconnect them to those who dreamt them up.

After the Chiefs lifted the trophy, the group went out to celebrate, and clips showed him partying in a club afterward. In it, he can be seen behind a set of DJ decks while wearing a wrestling mask.

A celebration that could get him in trouble?

With the videos of Jason Kelce partying hard following his 34-year-old brother’s Super Bowl win, we wonder if it will get him in any amount of trouble. This follows a recent incident in which Jason ripped off his shirt and screamed in delight when Travis scored a touchdown in a game. The moment was caught on camera and shared on Instagram. Then, in one of their podcast episodes, Travis teased him about it.

Travis said “Never once been on his Instagram?” referring to their dad never having posted his son on his Instagram until he was shirtless in celebration.

“Listen kids, if you want to make your dad proud, take your shirt off, drink your beer and scream on the top of your lungs and apparently that’s what does it,” Jason responded.

Proud of his older sibling, Travis said: “That’s my boy!”

The post in question was captioned “Jason celebrating Trav’s score,” and had many people talking in the comments.

One person penned: “Jason is the ultimate hype man and a fantastic big brother!!!”

Another said: “Your sons are great guys, man! This is gold [raising hands emoji].”

“Amazing moment! We love to see it!!” wrote someone else.

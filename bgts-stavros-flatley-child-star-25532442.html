BGT's Stavros Flatley child star unrecognisable with beard and tattoos 12 years later

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Britain's Got Talent stars Stavros Flatley have not been forgotten about all these years later - and the father/son duo still work together now.

However, Demetrios Demetriou and his son Michalakis - nicknamed Lagi - still look different 12 years later, particularly son Lagi, who appeared on the show when he was just 13-years-old.

Now, aged 25, Lagi looks unrecognisable, but he hasn't let his part in Stavros Flatley fade into the distance.

Lagi now boasts a thick beard and countless tattoos, including the ever noticeable map of Cyrpus which acted as a trademark for his dad as they performed for the first time.

Both Demetrios and Lagi boast Cyprus related tattoos as part of their impressive gig, but Lagi has certainly grown up since his original debut.

Alongside a beard and tattoos, Lagi also has a trim figure with the dancer showing off his workout routines through his profile.

It's certainly different from his initial debut back in 2009.

Lagi, who of course had no tattoos on his skin as he was so young, hit the stage with a blonde wig alongside his dad with matching hair and his ink.

To remember the impressive stint, Lagi has even got ink of his dad on him from their debut on Britain's Got Talent.

While he looks different, it seems the act hasn't been put on a back burner with Lagi sharing multiple snaps on his Instagram of himself and his dad showing off their tats and clips of them.

He has also joked that often when he goes out for dinner people will come up to him and urge him to "do the dance".

Lagi has information regarding enquiries for the dancing duo on his profile as he confirmed that they're still taking bookings.

Elsewhere, Lagi has hit a new high in his personal life too with the dancer popping the question to his long-term girlfriend earlier this year.

The husband and wife-to-be have also announced sweet news that they're having a baby together.

Fans and friends flooded them with messages of congratulations after they posted a video to announce their happy life update.

Kendall Jenner Proves a Tropical Beach Wardrobe Can Be All Black

Publish date: 2024-02-27

Kendall Jenner is validating my belief that any wardrobe, for any weather, can be composed of all-black staples.

The supermodel today shared an Instagram photo dump from a recent relaxing beach vacation, looking like the chicest tropical goddess in a black summer maxi dress by Johanna Ortiz—a Colombian designer known for her feminine, beachy designs.

The piece features a graphic print of two hibiscus flowers (a typical Hawaiian mofit), in creamy white and pastel pink.

Jenner actually wore the piece backwards, styling the halterneck back as the front, and leaving the plunging V neckline and diamond-shaped torso cutout in the back. Somehow, it still worked.

You can shop the piece for $1,250, and see its original styling, on FWRD.

Jenner wore the flowy dress only with classic gold hoop earrings, and she styled her hair in a messy, saltwater-wet bun. She seemed to wear no makeup as she posed barefoot in the minimalist dress beside a tall palm tree, during a sleepy evening walk on an empty beach.

“Twins,” she wrote in the caption, referring to the first photo in her Instagram slideshow, where she stands tall with her long arms stretched out over her head, like a sophisticated palm tree.

Jenner added in the caption that she found her dress on FWRD, the retail site that named her creative director in 2021. The supermodel regularly drops curated lists of her favorite items to shop on the site—this elegant vacation LBD evidently being one of them.

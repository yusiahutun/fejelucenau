YFN Lucci’s Bio

Born in Atlanta, Georgia the 16th of February 1991, YFN Lucci - age 28 - is an American by nationality. However, his parents are Jamaican - who had immigrated to the United States. The rapper possesses African-American ethnicity, derived from his parents.

Net Worth In 2019

YFN Lucci shot to fame after the release of his mixtape, Wish Me Well. The rapper began his career in the music industry at the age of 17 after building a close relationship with Johnny Cinco.

In December 2014, the rapper signed a record deal with the record label, Think It’s A Game Entertainment and released his debut mixtape, Wish Me Well. The album was a huge hit and made him a well-known artist in the industry.

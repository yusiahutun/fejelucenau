Coastal Federal Credit Union Signs On For Naming Rights At Walnut Creek

Publish date: 2024-03-09

RALEIGH, N.C. (CelebrityAccess) — Walnut Creek Amphitheater in Raleigh has signed a multi-year naming rights deal with Coastal Federal Credit Union that will see the venue renamed as Coastal Credit Union Music Park at Walnut Creek.

As a part of the deal at the amphitheater, Coastal Federal members will have access to a 'fast-pass' lane at the venue and a special hospitality tent. Additional benefits were expected to be announced.

Managed by Live Nation on behalf of the City of Raleigh, the 20,000-capacity venue sees around 300,000 concert-goers a year attending shows by acts such as James Taylor, Kayne West and Aerosmith.

"I'm thrilled by this unique opportunity to strengthen Coastal's brand and to engage our community in a new, entertaining way," Coastal President and Chief Executive Chuck Purvis said in a statement. – Staff Writers

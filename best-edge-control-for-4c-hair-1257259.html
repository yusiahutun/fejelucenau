    FAQ's

    Is edge control stronger than gel?

    Edge controls are similar to pomades and thus offer a much stronger hold to your hairline than gel.

    Can you use edge control every day?

    It is not really recommended to use an edge control every day as it might lead to build-up and result in hair breakage.

    Can edge control help with baby hair and flyaways?

    Yes, and edge control definitely helps in laying down baby hair and flyaways.

    Can I use edge control on wet or damp hair?

    It is recommended to use edge control on damp hair.

Which Dragon Breed is the smallest in Hogwarts Legacy? All the details

Publish date: 2024-01-26

Hogwarts Legacy is known for its wide range of puzzles and side quests. While some are directly linked to well-known pieces of lore, others are not. Sophronia Franklin's series of quizzes, including which dragon breed is the smallest, is considered the most challenging in the game's activities.

The Peruvian Vipertooth is the smallest known dragon but the swiftest in flight. The fire-breathing creature is smooth-scaled, copper-coloured, and has black ridge markings. Although it is merely 15 feet long, it has particularly venomous fangs and short horns.

Which dragon breed is the smallest in Harry Potter?

The smallest dragon breed in Hogwarts Legacy is the Peruvian Vipertooth. The copper-coloured creature originated from eastern and northeastern Peru. It has a unique appearance thanks to its black ridge-marking.

Peruvian Vipertooth’s history

Towards the end of the 19th century, the Peruvian Vipertooth population increased alarmingly. As a result, they were seen as a serious threat to Muggles and wizards. The International Confederation of Wizards sent an extermination squad to reduce their population.

Harvey Ridgebit, a popular dragonologist, was the first to catch a Peruvian Vipertooth. Wizards who worked closely with this breed initially contracted the Dragon Pox disease, which became one of the most widespread wizarding diseases.

How big is a Peruvian Vipertooth?

According to Hogwarts is Here, the Peruvian Vipertooth is only 15 feet long. It is almost a third of the Hungarian Horntails fire range. Due to its small size, it can easily conceal itself from the Muggles and other attackers.

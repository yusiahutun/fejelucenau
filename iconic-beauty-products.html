The 100 Most Iconic Beauty Products of All Time

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Beauty may thrive on trends, but the most exciting products are the ones that artists, experts, and obsessives return to over and over again. What earns them such loyalty? They perform better than the versions that came before them, and in many cases created a dent in the beauty universe: YSL Beauty created the light-reflecting concealer; La Mer upped the bar on luxury face cream; Calvin Klein made genderless scent the norm. Every fragrance, formula, and shade here is a classic for a reason. Ahead, shop our list of the 100 most iconic beauty products of all time.

— SKINCARE ICONS—

La Prairie Skin Caviar Luxe Cream

La Prairie’s iconic caviar-infused cream redefined decadence in the skincare space. 

Bioderma Sensibio H2O

What was once the worst-kept French pharmacy secret is now the most beloved micellar cleansing water you can buy almost anywhere. 

Tatcha The Rice Polish Foaming Enzyme Powder

Now 25% Off

This gentle exfoliating powder leaves your skin so glowy, one might mistake you for Meghan, Duchess of Sussex (it’s rumored that she’s a fan). 

Estēe Lauder Advanced Night Repair Serum Synchronized Multi-Recovery Complex

It’s not often we can call a nighttime serum a worldwide phenomenon, but Lauder’s ANR is precisely that. 

Fresh Soy Makeup Removing Face Wash

Combination and oily skin havers stock up on this face wash, because it wipes away all signs of makeup and oil without leaving skin irritated or stripped.

CeraVe Daily Moisturizing Lotion

Dermatologists and TikTok agree: This basic, no-frills moisturizer just makes your skin feel so fresh and healthy.

Clinique Dramatically Different Moisturizing Lotion+

The pale-yellow shade, the packaging, the skin-coddling formula—Clinique’s moisturizer for all skin types is a Hall of Famer.

Lancôme Bi-Facil Double-Action Eye Makeup Remover

Waterproof mascara and apocalypse-proof lipstick are no match for this genius makeup remover.

Caudalie Beauty Elixir

Before setting sprays and skin mists, there was just this: a skin-toning and radiance-boosting mist that feels like a trip to the spa.

Biologique Recherche Lotion P50

There are exfoliating toners—and then there’s P50. In a league of its own, Biologique Recherche Lotion P50 resurfaces skin for a major glow.

Kiehl's Since 1851 Crème de Corps

Squalane and cocoa butter team up to leave your skin impossibly soft and healed.

Dr. Dennis Gross Skincare Alpha Beta Universal Daily Peel

These peel pads are the next best thing to a chemical peel at your derm’s office.

Cetaphil Daily Facial Cleanser
Credit: Amazon

We keep coming back to this classic cleanser because it simply gets the job done. No gimmicks, just results. 

Aveeno Daily Moisturizing Body Lotion

Now 27% Off

The blend of skin-soothing oat extract with dimethicone helps deeply moisturize dry skin and protect it.

Jergens Natural Glow +FIRMING Self Tanner Lotion

Now 14% Off

This gradual self-tanner has had several upgrades and updates over the years, but it’s still our go-to for a subtle boost of color.

St. Tropez Self Tan Classic Bronzing Mousse

Now 24% Off

Though the brand has an array of innovative formulas, this classic self-tanning mousse from St. Tropez delivers an even, gorgeous glow every time.

La Mer The Moisturizing Soft Cream

La Mer Crème de la Mer leaves skin feeling pampered, supple, in short—expensive.

Olay Regenerist Micro Sculpting Cream

Olay Regenerist Micro-Sculpting Cream Moisturizer plumps and lifts.

Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Skin Protectant

Elizabeth Arden Eight Hour Cream Skin Protectant can be slathered on lips, cuticles, eyes, cheeks—you name it.

SK-II Facial Treatment Essence

SK-II Facial Essence is the first step in any skin care routine—the watery lotion is packed with more than 50 vitamins and minerals.

Thayers Alcohol-Free Witch Hazel Facial Toner

Thayers Alcohol-Free Witch Hazel Facial Toner refreshes without drying (and has been doing so for nearly 200 years).

Pond's Cold Cream Cleanser

Now 18% Off

Pond’s Cold Cream removes makeup while imparting softness.

Nivea Crème

Slather this on your hands, face, body—wherever you need a dose of serious moisture and TLC.

— FACE MAKEUP ICONS—

NARS Radiant Creamy Concealer

This iconic concealer lives up to its name: It’s creamy, it’s radiant, and it’s the only tube we reach for when we need to look well slept and alive. There’s a reason it’s the number one concealer in the country.

Yves Saint Laurent Touche Éclat All-Over Brightening Concealer Pen

This sheer, radiance-boosting concealer changed the entire beauty industry for the better. To makeup artists and beauty obsessives, Touche Èclat is a full sentence. 

Cle de Peau Beaute Concealer SPF 25

If you’ve ever had a pimple, you’ve probably heard of this award-winning concealer from beloved Japanese luxury brand Clé de Peau. The unique, creamy texture is like Photoshop for spots. 

Laura Mercier Secret Camouflage Concealer

The secret to Secret Camouflage’s success is that it offers two shades of concealer, so you can always find the exact hue you need to cover dark marks, spots, redness, and more.

Armani Beauty Luminous Silk Foundation

Giorgio Armani Luminous Silk Foundation is a staple in almost every makeup artist’s kit: It goes on sheer and looks fresh even hours after applying.

Estēe Lauder Double Wear Stay-In-Place Foundation

Estée Lauder Double Wear Stay-in-Place Foundation comes in nearly 60 shades and has unmatched staying power.

Laura Mercier Translucent Loose Setting Powder

Lightweight, long-wearing, and skin-perfecting, this translucent powder outperforms all the imitators. 

Maybelline Instant Age Rewind Eraser Dark Circle Treatment Concealer

This drugstore staple redefined the full-coverage concealer. A few taps and you’ll forget dark circles even exist. 

— EYEBROW ICONS—

Anastasia Beverly Hills Brow Wiz

Everything we know about eyebrows we owe to Anastasia Soare from Anastasia Beverly Hills. To many beauty obsessives, this is the only brow pencil to exist.

Benefit Cosmetics Goof Proof Waterproof Easy Shape & Fill Eyebrow Pencil

This fan-favorite pencil has an angled tip for perfect shaping. Plus, it blends out like a dream.

Glossier Boy Brow

Boy Brow launched in 2015 during the height of our obsession with Cara Delevingne–inspired brows. Unlike other gels that simply set hairs in place, the tinted waxy-pomade texture makes brows appear instantly fluffier.

— MASCARA ICONS—

Lancôme Hypnôse Volumizing Mascara

Now 50% Off

If length and volume are what you’re after, pick up a tube of this fan-favorite mascara. 

Dior Diorshow Mascara

Dior Diorshow delivers volume you can see from the runway, and it won’t smudge or flake.

Maybelline Great Lash Mascara

Maybelline New York Great Lash uses a teeny spiral spoolie to impressively comb through every eyelash.

L'Oréal Voluminous Original Mascara

As the name suggests, this is the only mascara to turn to if you’re after good, clean volume. It’s a staple in makeup artist's kits. 

Too Faced Better Than Sex Volumizing Mascara

The name’s a little hyperbolic, but the results aren’t: If dramatic lashes are your thing, this mascara delivers iconic results.

Chanel Le Volume De Chanel Mascara

Volume is in the name, but this mascara also helps define each lash and hold curl.

Benefit Cosmetics They're Real! Lengthening Mascara

This mascara is all about length and definition, and the brush helps capture every single lash.

— CHEEK ICONS—

NARS Blush in Orgasm

A delicate balance of peach, pink, and gold, NARS Orgasm works on all skin tones (and is just fun to say).

Benefit Cosmetics Hoola Bronzer

This makeup staple delivers the perfect sun-kissed matte color for all skin tones.

Bobbi Brown Cosmetics Shimmer Brick Compact

Bobbi Brown’s Shimmer Brick Highlighter has been creating a candlelit glow since before highlighter became part of our standard makeup vocabulary. 

Chanel Les Beiges Healthy Glow Bronzing Cream

A perennial favorite of makeup artists everywhere, this unique gel-cream bronzer leaves behind the most natural-looking glow.

NARS Bronzing Powder in Laguna

Now 30% Off

Bronzer is its own language. There’s Hoola, and then there’s Laguna, a shimmery powder that blends like a dream.

Guerlain Terracotta Original Bronzing Powder

This oversized bronzer doesn’t just make you glow—it makes you look like you just stepped off a yacht.

Guerlain Météorites Highlighting Powder Pearls

From the handcrafted illuminating pearls to the signature violet scent, this highlighter is truly iconic in every way.

Tom Ford Soleil Glow Bronzer

Only a bronze icon could create such an iconic bronzer. Silky, soft, and skin-loving, this bronzer makes you feel more expensive with every swipe.

Benefit Cosmetics Lip & Cheek Stain Tint in Benetint

This stain was originally invented for, um, not your face, but has since become known as the go-to lip and cheek stain.

— LIP ICONS—

Too Faced Lip Injection Plumping Lip Gloss

It definitely tingles, but this plumping lip gloss leaves your lips looking instantly lush.

MAC Retro Matte Lipstick in Ruby Woo

There’s no other red lipstick in the world that inspires such devotion, because Ruby Woo looks great on everyone.

Clinique Almost Lipstick in Black Honey

Black Honey works with the tone of your lips to leave the most gorgeous natural berry sheen.

Lancôme Juicy Tubes Original Lip Gloss

Juicy Tubes are mostly responsible for the lip gloss craze of the early 2000s, but the decadent formula still holds up decades later.

Revlon Super Lustrous Lipstick in Fire & Ice

This bright-red lipstick has been turning heads since the 1950s, and it still looks gorgeous today.

Dior Addict Lip Maximizer Plumping Gloss

This gloss gets credit for seriously plumping and hydrating without feeling sticky. 

ChapStick Classic Lip Balm Tube, 8-Hour Moisture Cherry

Cherry ChapStick was a classic even before Katy Perry immortalized it in song. 

Burt's Bees Moisturizing Lip Balm

You know a classic when you see one. In the case of Burt’s Bees balms, we see them rolling around the bottom of every handbag we’ve ever owned. 

Dior Rouge Dior Refillable Lipstick in 999 Velvet

Other than five, 999 is probably the most recognizable number in the beauty world. The red lipstick from Dior is universally flattering.

NARS Lipstick in Schiap

Named after Elsa Schiaparelli’s signature hue, Schiap is the bold lipstick shade you’ve seen on nearly every celebrity.

Maybelline New York Baby Lips Moisturizing Lip Balm

From the packaging to the formula, Maybelline’s iconic Baby Lips balm is a drugstore beauty staple. 

MAC Lipglass Clear

Though many clear lip glosses exist these days, there’s only one makeup artists recognize: M.A.C’s Lipglass. 

— HAIR ICONS—

Oribe Dry Texturizing Spray

This cult-favorite texturizing spray adds body and volume without weight. When it launched, it kick-started an entirely new category in hair care. 

Head and Shoulders Shampoo in Classic Clean

This is the only shampoo we turn to when our scalps are irritated and flakes start falling.

Pantene Pro-V Classic Clean Shampoo

This deliciously scented shampoo gently cleanses to remove product buildup, resulting in your hair looking soft and bouncy. 

Tresemme TRES Two Extra Hold Hair Spray

Chances are you’ve probably owned a bottle of this hairspray at one point in your life. It holds for hours, quells frizz, and can still be styled and brushed for a softer look. 

Dyson Supersonic Hair Dryer

In the sacred beauty timeline, there is B.D. (before Dyson) and A.D. (after Dyson). The brand changed the way we approach heat styling and damage from hot tools. 

Klorane Dry Shampoo with Oat Milk

The first time we ever heard of oat milk wasn’t in a coffee shop but in this award-winning dry shampoo. It cleanses hair, eliminates grease, and adds texture and volume. 

Paul Mitchell Original Awapuhi Shampoo

Now 34% Off

Nothing tackles serious oil while keeping hair hydrated like Paul Mitchell’s Awapuhi. Plus, it smells so damn good.

Aveda Rosemary Mint Purifying Shampoo

Micelles and white vinegar purify your hair and scalp, while the (certified organic) rosemary and peppermint fragrance feels like a jolt of caffeine in the morning.

Wet Brush Original Hair Brush in Black

Now 17% Off

Soft, flexible bristles help glide through tangles without tugging or pulling. This brush is an essential in every bathroom.

Tangle Teezer The Original Detangling Hair Brush

For serious knots and tangles, grab the Tangle Teezer. The unique palm-shaped design helps you tackle even stubborn, hard-to-reach knots on wet or dry hair.

Denman D3 Original 7-Row Styling Brush, Gloss Black

This brush styles wet curls flawlessly, but can also be used for detangling and blow-drying. 

Moroccanoil Moroccanoil Treatment

This argan oil-infused serum and its iconic scent have kept our hair smooth, shiny, and smelling good for years. 

L'Oréal Elnett Satin Extra Strong Hold Unscented Hairspray

Once only available overseas, L’Oréal’s Elnett hairspray has been flying off shelves in the United States for years because of its ultra-fine mist and brushable hold. 

Kérastase Nutritive Shampoo Bain Satin 1

This shampoo gently cleanses hair, adds shine, and boosts health with antioxidants to prevent damage. 

— FRAGRANCE ICONS—

Viktor&Rolf Flowerbomb

This warm, sexy scent helped catapult floral perfumes into the modern era. 

Chanel N°5 Eau de Parfum

Chanel No. 5’s extravagant floral and aldehydic notes are just part of the reason the eau de parfum influenced the entire beauty industry. Shrouded in myth and Old Hollywood glamour, owning a bottle of No. 5 is still a rite of passage for all beauty fans.

Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium Eau de Parfum

This modern take on a similar fragrance from the 1970s features popular notes of black coffee, white flowers, and sweet vanilla. 

Dior Miss Dior Eau de Parfum

Like many icons, Miss Dior is a master of reinvention. The latest iteration features a spicier rose, as well as subtle notes of peach and apricot. 

LE LABO Santal 33 Eau de Parfum

There was a point in time that the entirety of Manhattan smelled like Santal 33.  

Prada Candy Eau de Parfum

Much like candy, this sweet-but-elegant scent is addictive in the best way.

Mugler Angel Eau de Parfum

The first modern gourmand perfume, Angel set the tone for the so-sweet-you-could-eat-it fragrances that still line our vanities today.

Marc Jacobs Fragrances Daisy

Fresh and floral with a girlish charm, Daisy is for those who are young or just young at heart.

Guerlain Shalimar Eau de Parfum

In the 1920s, wearing Shalimar meant you had a bad-girl reputation. Today, it means you have excellent taste and an appreciation for fragrance classics.

Calvin Klein CK One

This deliciously scented shampoo gently cleanses to remove product buildup, resulting in your hair looking soft and bouncy. 

Dior J'adore Eau de Parfum

The bottle, the name, the scent, the advertisements—everything J’adore touches is iconic.

Elizabeth Taylor White Diamonds Eau de Toilette Spray

You can thank White Diamonds, a sheer floral, for the hundreds (thousands?) of celebrity fragrances that followed after it dropped in 1991. 

Tom Ford Black Orchid Eau de Parfum

It’s hard to pick just one iconic Tom Ford scent, but Black Orchid was the designer’s first release back in 2006. It’s a must-try for fragrance lovers. 

HERMÈS Terre d’Hermès Eau de Toilette

A classic woodsy scent that anyone can wear well. 

— TOOL ICONS—

Shu Uemura Iconic Eyelash Curler

Shu Uemura Eyelash Curler is the only lash curler as far as most makeup artists are concerned; it allows you to get super close to the lash line.

Tweezerman Stainless Steel Slant Tweezer

Tweezerman’s superior quality and engineering result in tweezers that will stay sharp and precise for years to come.

Beautyblender Original Makeup Sponge

The Beautyblender Original is the perfect shape for buffing foundation into an airbrushed finish. Simply dampen and squeeze before using.

— NAIL ICONS—

OPI Nail Lacquer Nail Polish in Lincoln Park After Dark

This moody, near-black purple is our go-to fall manicure shade. 

CND Solar Oil Nail and Cuticle Conditioner

This blend of lightweight oils helps heal scraggly, dry cuticles within minutes. 

Essie Nail Lacquer in Wicked

Another near-black, Wicked is a chic bloodred shade. 

Essie Nail Lacquer in Mademoiselle

This sheer pink leaves nails looking glossy and refined without adding much color at all.

Essie Nail Lacquer in Ballet Slippers

Beloved by royals, Ballet Slippers is the prettiest shade of pale pink. 

OPI Nail Lacquer Nail Polish in Big Apple Red

Nothing’s more iconic than a red manicure, and Big Apple Red packs a punch every time.

Chanel Le Vernis Longwear Nail Colour in Vamp

This nearly-black red polish hit the scene in 1994 and started a frenzy of similar shades.

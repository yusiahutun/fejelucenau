Vardy v Rooney viewers fuming as they slam 'diabolical' accent in Channel 4 drama

Publish date: 2024-09-26

Channel 4's Wagatha Christie drama - Vardy v Rooney - has faced a bitter backlash from viewers slating the show's Scouse accent.

The two-part series has brought to life the sensational real-life High Court libel trial between rival WAGs Coleen Rooney and Rebekah Vardy which took place earlier this year.

The whole nation was gripped as the footballers' wives battled it out in the courtroom for seven days in May, with intimate details of their private lives laid out bare in the courtroom.

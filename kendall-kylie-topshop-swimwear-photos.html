    Kendall + Kylie

    Tomorrow, Kendall and Kylie Jenner's eponymous line returns to Topshop with a hot swimwear collection. The sexy-meets-athletic suits were inspired by their native Cali environment. "For our new swimwear collection, we drew inspiration from our California roots; every look captures the beachy summer vibe of LA," Kendall said in a press release. The result is a 37-piece range of bikinis, one-pieces and cover-ups that are "perfect for every occasion," as Kylie said. Flip through to get a preview of some of the best pieces, and get ready to shop them online and in-store at Topshop tomorrow.

    Kendall + Kylie bathing suits, $35-$85, topshop.com. Available June 9.

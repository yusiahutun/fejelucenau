Nikki Mudarris Career

In 2014, Nikki Mudarris made her debut as a supporting cast member on the reality TV show ‘Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood’. As Season 2 rolled around, fans were surprised to learn that Nikki had broken things off with Lil Fizz.

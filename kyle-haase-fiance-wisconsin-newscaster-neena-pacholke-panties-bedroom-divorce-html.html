EXCLUSIVE: Fianc of Wisconsin newscaster Neena Pacholke - who killed herself after finding another

Publish date: 2024-07-21

The fiancé of the Wisconsin newscaster who killed herself last year just weeks after her wedding was called off, got engaged, married and now is getting a divorce DailyMail.com can exclusively reveal.

Beloved local Wausau reporter, Neena Pacholke, 27, shot herself in August 2022 after her then-fiancé, Kyle Haase, 39, called off their wedding.

But the 'grieving' fiancé wasted no time getting back into the dating scene. Just months after Neena's death, Kyle was seen at a local store with a former lover – the same woman that Neena told friends she suspected he was cheating with and whose panties she found in their bedroom.

Months later, Kyle met 23-year-old Ashley Groshek out at a local bar and they hit it off instantly, a source told DailyMail.com.  

The relationship moved at warp speed, culminating in an engagement in June and a September 9 wedding in Hatley, Wisconsin, attended by family and friends.

Now DailyMail.com has obtained court papers that show Kyle and Ashley jointly filed for divorce on October 18 after less than six weeks of marriage.

'Kyle got engaged, married and now is divorcing all in a little over a year since his ex-fiancée took her own life,' said a friend of Neena's, who asked not to be identified. 

'His actions speak louder than words. I'm not sure how someone can fall in love, get married and file for divorce in that short of time after experiencing the loss of a fiancée. It's obvious the lack of respect he had for Neena.'

Haase, who has two children from a previous marriage, still lives in the same house where Neena took her life in a bedroom. They had bought it together a month earlier. Kyle even lived in the house with his new bride for several weeks.

'I'm glad Ashley is getting away from Kyle, for her own sanity.  I just hope and pray Ashley is going to be okay,' a friend said 

Ashley is from nearby Amherst Junction, Wisconsin and has an associate degree in Agribusiness- Animal Science from Fox Valley Technical College, according to her LinkedIn page. She currently works as a reproductive technician at a dairy company. 

Much like Haase's tumultuous two-year relationship with Pacholke, his relationship with Groshek was marred with fights and altercations. 

At a KISS concert in Crandon, Wisconsin, on September 1 – eight days before their wedding – sources say both Ashley and Kyle were drinking when Ashley went through his phone and saw that he had been texting other women. She became angry and attacked him.

Both Kyle and Ashley denied this event. They both emailed DailyMal.com with the exact same words: 'None of that is true,' while Ashley added: 'Kyle has always been loyal to me.'

Then just days before they were scheduled to be married, Ashley's bridal shower was cancelled at the last minute with no explanation.

Kyle went to great lengths in keeping the details of his relationship with Neena under wraps from Ashley, added the pal.

When Kyle found out that Neena's family was flying from Tampa to Wausau to celebrate her on her birthday on June 29 with her friends, Kyle took Ashley out of town for a 'surprise weekend' on a lake just so she wouldn't run into Neena's parents.

The weekend before Neena's birthday Kyle asked Ashley to marry him. A source says the ring he gave Ashley looks like the one he gave Neena, even questioning that it may have been the same ring, with minor modifications.

The split came after a tumultuous two-year romance filled with fights, arrests, heaving drinking and rumors of affairs.

DailyMail.com obtained the full police report which details the efforts police and family members went to in the hours before she took her own life.

A worried friend had even called 911 and asked police to do a welfare check, but by the time they got there, Neena was already gone.

The report details how several officers went to Neena's home on the morning of August 27 after she had made a number of suicidal comments in text messages.

The day before, a friend had removed all firearms from the home for fear of Neena's precarious mental state.

Fiancé Kyle was on his way to Minnesota to see his mother along with his children but spoke to Neena who told him that she was 'going to do something,' however she was not specific.

Kyle then contacted the police asking them to do a welfare check on Neena's home. He advised them of the code for the garage so the police could get inside. 

When police went to the home at 11.20am there was no response.

Suddenly they heard a noise and clattering as if something had fallen. Police waited for around half an hour before finally forcing a door open.

Officers went into a bedroom where the smell of gunpowder lingered in the air.

They found Neena in a closet leaning against the wall with a firearm laying next to her right hand on the ground.

There was also a mark in the wall where the bullet had passed through her skull into the drywall.

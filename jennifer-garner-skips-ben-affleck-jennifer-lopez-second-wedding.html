Where Jennifer Garner Was During Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez's Second Wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-25

On Jennifer Lopez and Ben Affleck's second wedding day, Jennifer Garner was running errands with family.

The 13 Going on 30 star was spotted on a shopping trip at a Sam's Club location in South Charleston, West Virginia, Saturday afternoon, per TMZ. She wore a blue tee and stripped linen pants as she browsed the box store with two men who appeared to be her father and her boyfriend, businessman John Miller, taking time to snap pictures with fans.

Page Six previously reported that the Alias alum skipped the weekend of festivities due to a scheduling conflict, while her children with Affleck—Violet, 16, Seraphina, 13, and Samuel, 10—attended the ceremony on the Good Will Hunting actor's Riceboro, Georgia, estate.

Ever the private star, Garner has not commented on either of the couple's weddings.

The actress has reportedly been a supportive co-parent and ex throughout the Bennifer-2.0 era. On the day of the couple's first wedding, Garner was on a relaxing solo trip at Lake Tahoe. Page Six previously reported that Garner was made aware of Bennifer's marriage plans a day before the Las Vegas ceremony.

"Ben did tell Jen about the wedding, but I'm told that it was only Friday at the earliest. It was very, very minimally planned in advance and was largely spur-of-the-moment," a source told the outlet, adding that Affleck and Garner's daughter Violet "stayed home because she's extremely loyal to her mom."

Following the whirlwind wedding, a source for Entertainment Tonight shared how Garner, who was married to Affleck from 2005 to 2018, and Marc Anthony, who was married to Lopez from 2004 to 2014, were processing the milestone.

"Marc Anthony and Jen and Ben and Jen Garner all still get along relatively well and share special places in each other's hearts," the source said. "There is no ill will from anyone and the mutual goal and focus is zero drama and to prioritize being great parents to their children."

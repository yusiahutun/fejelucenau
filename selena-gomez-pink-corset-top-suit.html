Selena Gomez Is a Hollywood Barbie in a Bright Pink Corset-Top Suit

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Selena Gomez is a Hollywood Barbie.

The singer and actor attended the Music + Health Summit at West Hollywood’s 1 Hotel last night, looking glamorous in head-to-toe pink.

Her bright suit look was composed of a silky corset top tucked into pleated trousers and paired with an oversize blazer with clear brown buttons. Gomez added even more color with a pair of shimmering black pointed-toe heels with colorful flower embellishments all over, by Betsey Johnson. For extra shimmer, she accessorized with a diamond choker and large silver hoop earrings also encrusted with diamonds.

Gomez continued the glamour with her makeup: bronzy shadow, dark liner, rosy cheeks, and a classic red lip (potentially using her recently released Rare Beauty matte red lipstick). Her hair was slicked back into a tight bun.

Michael Buckner//Getty Images

The “Calm Down” singer recently showed off another bold, colorful look while at the 2023 MTV VMAs. She made an entrance at the star-studded award show in a vibrant red spring gown by Oscar de la Renta. The stunning piece was composed entirely of flower-shaped appliqués that fell into a fringe train, which swished romantically as she walked, and included a plunging balconette bra neckline and a high leg slit. Gomez let the gown shine, pairing it only with strappy red sandals, white diamond floral earrings, and a matching diamond floral ring. Her glam for the evening included a slicked-down straight hairdo, a bronze smoky eye, rosy makeup, and a matte brownish-pink lip.

The History of Macclesfield Town FC

Macclesfield Town FC, also known as the Silkmen, is a historic football club based in Macclesfield, Cheshire, England. The club was formed in 1874 and has since become a significant part of the town’s sporting heritage.

The Club’s Stadium

Macclesfield Town FC plays its home matches at Moss Rose Stadium. With a seating capacity of 6,355, this iconic stadium has been the home ground of the club since 1891.

Macclesfield Town FC’s Nickname

The team is commonly referred to as the Silkmen due to Macclesfield’s historical association with the silk industry. The nickname is a nod to the town’s heritage and the role it played in the textile trade during the Industrial Revolution.

Paul Greene’s Bio

A native of Wetaskiwin, Alberta, Canada, Paul Greene was born on 2nd June 1974. His star sign is Gemini. By nationality, Greene is Canadian, having a white North American ethnic background. Not much known about his parent and siblings. Also, he has revealed any information about his academic background.

He came international attention for a role in the My NetworkTV telenovela Wicked Wicked Games, where he was a series regular appearing in forty episodes as Benjamin Gray. Furthermore, his other acting credits include guest roles in series such as NCIS, My Own Worst Enemy, Freddie, The Wedding Bells, and Shark.

Additionally, he has appeared in over 100 television commercials. And most recently, he appeared in TV series When Calls the Heart, playing the role Dr. Carson Shepard.

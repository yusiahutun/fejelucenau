Who Is Robert Sandberg? Is He Still Together With Mia Khalifa?

Publish date: 2024-03-28

Robert Sandberg is a multi-award-winning chef working at the 3 Michelin-starred Frantzén restaurants in Stockholm. He is also known as the ex-husband of the former adult star, media personality, and webcam model Mia Khalifa.

Sandberg has also practiced and worked as a chef at Noma restaurant. World Chefs’ Cup winner Robert Sandberg has worked in some of the world’s finest kitchens for more than 15 years. He is the winner of the 2016 Hans Bueschken Young Chefs Competition in  Thessaloniki (Greece) and the Junior World Cup.

He has also worked in Los Angeles and has gained over a million Instagram followers while working in the prestigious kitchens mentioned above. Keep scrolling to find out more about his cooking career, his relationship with ex-wife Mia Khalifa and even his leftover food.

Robert Sandberg Birth Details, Parents, And Siblings 

Nordic chef Robert Sandberg was born to parents Monia Sandberg and Hans Sandberg on 17 January 1993. The 29-year-old chef was born in Norrmalm, Stockholm Lane, Sweden.

Sandberg is apparently the only son of his father, Hans Johan Sandberg, and his mother, Monia Sandberg. All his siblings are sisters; they are Anna Sandberg, Fredrik Tagliatelle Sandberg, and Julle Sandberg.

How Did The Professional Chef, Robert Begin His Culinary Career?

As mentioned, over the years Sandberg has worked in several world-class restaurants such as Noma in Copenhagen and Maaemo in Oslo. Robert has been passionate about cooking since childhood.

Sandberg studied at the Falkenberg Hotel and Restaurant School in Sweden. The tattooed ex-husband of Khalifa started his culinary journey after working as a culinary intern in Paris.

His professional cooking career began in April 2012 when he started working as a chef at Noma restaurant. A year later, he took up the same position at Maaemo.

Then, in April 2013, Robert joined the Swedish Culinary Team as Executive Chef and has been a member ever since. He first joined the SWEDISH JUNIOR CULINARY TEAM in 2011. In May 2014, Sandberg became Sous Chef at Restaurant Frantzan, and three years later he became one of the Executive Chefs at Kong Hans Kaelder in Copenhagen, Denmark.

In addition to the above, Sandberg is co-owner of Habitué in Los Angeles, working with Frantzen Chef Marcus Jernmark and wine professional Max Coane. Sandberg has also worked at Silver Bough, an eight-seat restaurant known for serving the most expensive food money can buy on the entire menu.

Sandberg And Mia Khalifa Married Each Other In Kitchen

12 famous blue cartoon characters that you should check out

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Blue is one of the most beloved colours in the world. Naturally, if you love this colour, you will be obsessed with cartoon characters that are blue in colour. The anime creators have not disappointed fans of this colour as they have included blue cartoon characters in movies. But which are the most famous blue characters?

Since their inception, cartoons have become an integral part of storytelling in the anime world. What differentiates these characters from human actors is their appearance. Unlike the human characters, cartoons are at liberty to take on any skin colour that the creator wishes. For instance, there are many fictional cartoon characters that sport blue hues.

List of famous blue cartoon characters

Tons of blue characters have been featured in anime movies. However, some have stood out from the rest thanks to their outstanding performance. Here is a list of blue animated characters.

1. Dory (Finding Nemo)

Dory is one of the most famous blue female cartoon characters. She is a regal blue tang who suffers from short-term memory loss and is a bit ditzy. Dory is one of the two main characters in Finding Nemo. She yearns to be part of something bigger than herself. Dory is responsible for saving the day.

2. Road Runner (Road Runner)

Road Runner is an anthropomorphic bird. The cartoon character from Looney Tunes is renowned for his fast speed. Road Runner was created for Warner Bros. He first appeared in Fast and Furry-ous as the main antagonist.

3. Megamind (Megamind)

Megamind is a blue cartoon character renowned for appearing in the Megamind Disney series. He is a supervillain who eventually turns into a superhero to save Metro City. He can easily be distinguished from other characters because he has a very gigantic head.

4. Genie (Aladdin)

Genie is one of the Disney characters that are blue. Genie is one of the most popular and iconic characters in the Aladdin story. In the movie, Aladdin and his genie are both blue. The Genie lives in the magical lamp in the cave of wonders. He is a gigantic character that is actually bigger than an adult human being. Genie has unlimited shape-shifting capabilities.

5. Rainbow Dash (My Little Pony)

Rainbow Dash is one of the most famous light blue characters. She is the main character of My Little Pony: Friendship is Magic. The blue pegasus dow has a rainbow-colored mane and tail. Rainbow Dash loves getting out and about and lives with a tortoise named Tank.

Rainbow Dash lives in Ponyville. She flies fast and speaks in a loud voice. The blue pegasus is a member of The Best Pegasus Squad Ever.

6. James P. "Sulley" Sullivan (Monsters)

James is one of the most famous turquoise cartoon characters. He is renowned for his appearance in the Disney Pixar animated movie Monsters and its 2013 prequel. James is a large blue monster with some purple patches on his furs. Sulley is portrayed as a good-natured, hardworking, gentle, and confident character who loved his job as a Scarer.

7. Stitch (Lilo & Stitch)

Stitch is one of the most favourite cartoon characters. He is a blue alien from the Lilo & Stitch franchise. In the series, Stitch and his friend Lilo continuously find themselves in all sorts of trouble. Stitch is the least likeable character because of his violent and irritable behaviour.

8. Sonic the Hedgehog

Sonic is one of the most famous blue cartoon characters of the 90s. He is a blue hedgehog who can run at supersonic speeds. His iconic blue color is recognizable even from afar. Sonic has an incredible pace and rolls into a ball when attacking his enemies.

9. Buster Bunny

Buster Bunny is a cartoon character created in 1955 by Warner Bros. He first appeared in the animated short film Rabbit's Kin. Buster is the main character of the Looney Tunes and Merrie Melodies series of cartoons. He is renowned for the catchphrase, "That's all folks!"

10. The Smurfs

The Smurfs are blue-colored humanoids who live in mushroom houses. It's hard to single out one Smurf because they are not differentiated by name. These adorable little blue cartoon characters have won the audience's hearts.

11. Blueberry Muffin (Strawberry Shortcake)

Blueberry Muffin is one of the main characters in Strawberry Shortcake. She has featured in every generation so far. Blueberry Muffin is one of Strawberry Shortcake's best friends. Her classic outfit includes a blue dress and green-white leggings.

12. Bluey (Blue Heeler Puppy)

Bluey is a blue heeler pup who lives with her parents and sister. The pup loves to play and her parents and teachers give her lots of time to practice.

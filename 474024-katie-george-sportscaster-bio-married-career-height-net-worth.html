Educational background

ESPN's Katie George went to Assumption High School in Louisville, where she played volleyball on the girls’ team. She was an integral member of the team and was named Prep Volleyball National Player of the Year.

She proceeded to the University of Louisville after completing her high school education. She was in the university for three years and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Communications, with a minor in sports administration.

She was a volleyball player in the university and was among the most promising members of the Louisville Cardinals women’s volleyball team. She won two state championships and earned KVCA Kentucky Volleyball.

Career

When Katie was studying at the University of Louisville, her roommate suggested that she participate in the Miss Kentucky pageant. She did and won the Miss Kentucky USA 2015 title.

The win gave her entry into the Miss USA pageant, which was held at Raising Cane’s River Center Arena in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. She made it to the semifinals. Olivia Jordan from Oklahoma won the competition.

From Jan to May 2014, the sportscaster was a sports intern at WLKY Channel 32. She was also a productions intern at CBS Corporation from June to August 2014.

In January 2016, she joined WDRB News as a sports anchor and field reporter. She anchored the 11 pm sportscast on the network.

She was later hired by Fox Sports Wisconsin. She got the chance to cover the Milwaukee Bucks for the NBA season from 2018 to 2019.

She joined the ACC Network at its launch in 2019. She now serves as a reporter for ESPN/ABC Saturday college football games alongside Joe Tessitore and Jesse Palmer.

She also contributes to the coverage of several ESPN properties, including Premier Lacrosse, the XFL, and the NBA.

Is Katie George still with ESPN?

Yes, the sportscaster still works for ESPN. She hosts and moderates of ESPN’s Unlapped video podcast that covers Formula 1 racing.

She also hosts a race review programme for ESPN’s digital platforms after every F1 race. In addition, she contributes to ESPN digital preview shows for F1 races in America.

Is Katie George married?

The sportscaster got married on 21 January 2022. Her wedding was held at The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch, Colorado, United States of America.

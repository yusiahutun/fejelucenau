Kelly Cass Biography, Age, Height, Husband, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-04-03

Kelly Cass is an American weather reporter and a meteorologist famously known for her correspondence with AMHQ, The Weather Channel. She has had immense experience at her work and is highly skilled and respected in the field.

Early Life

Kelly Cass was born on 27th November 1973 and grew up in Poughkeepsie, New York, USA. She has not shared anything about her family, parents, or background but is known to be from a white catholic family. Growing up in her hometown, she naturally developed an interest in lakes effects and winter storms. She was also known to be highly athletic and played several sports during school. Kelly has completed her bachelor’s education in mass communication from Adelphi University in Long Island in New York. She has also attended Yonsei University in Seoul, South Korea from where she attained a certificate course in International study. She also holds a certificate in Broadcast Meteorology from Mississippi State University.

Kelly Cass Career

Kelly Cass started working as an on-camera meteorologist at WRGB-TV in New York. She worked there for seven years and meanwhile, she was also awarded the New York State Broadcasting Award for “big storm coverage” in 1993.  Later, she also worked as an associate producer and weeknight weathercaster at WTZA-TV in New York. In the year 2000, she joined The Weather Channel and started appearing on the channel on weekends as an on-camera meteorologist. She also presented a feature about being selected for a flight in FA18 jet in US Navy.

She is an approved meteorologist from National Weather Association and also holds full-time membership in the American Meteorological Society. Currently, she works at The Weather Channel and hosts weather on weekends on AMHQ. She also has her own website- Kellycass.com where she regularly posts her weather reports and interesting facts and information for her readers and followers. Along with this, she also is a Paleo food blogger.

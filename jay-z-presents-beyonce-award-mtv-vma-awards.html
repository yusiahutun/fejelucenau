Beyonc sheds tears of joy as she's presented with award by Jay Z and daughter Blue Ivy

Publish date: 2024-07-18

    Beyoncé was in for a surprise at the MTV video music awards in California on Sunday evening. Scroll down for the full list of winners.After a stunning 20-minute performance of a medley of songs from her latest album, queen Bey was presented with the Michael Jackson Vanguard Award by none other than her husband Jay Z and the couple's adorable daughter Blue Ivy.

    Rapper Jay Z stepped onstage with two-year-old Blue to say: "The recipient of the Michael Jackson Vanguard award and the greatest living entertainer Beyoncé."It was no doubt the joy of seeing her favourite duo that made the singer overcome with emotion. The star hugged and kissed her rapper husband and tiny tot after Jay Z handed over the prestigious paperweight.

    The overwhelmed star said: "I'm so full. Thank you. I have nothing to say but I'm filled with so much gratitude. I thank god for this moment. I love you so much. "Baby Blue, my beloved I love you." The couple's adorable daughter is then heard saying into the microphone: "Go Mommy".

    Beyoncé stole the show in a colourful mosaic leotard as she performed new hits such as and, as well as popular tracks such as and . The two-hour show featured other performances from artists including Taylor Swift, Usher and Nicky Minaj.

Charlize Theron Proves She Cannot Sing on Saturday Night Live: Video

Publish date: 2024-05-10

Charlize Theron has it all! Well, almost. The gorgeous actress is tall, beautiful, has won an Oscar, and even has an adorable son, but there’s one area she hasn’t mastered. 

The A Million Ways to Die in the West star can’t sing a note to save her life. On May 10 she hosted Saturday Night Live, where she reflected on her last time on the show in 2000 when she played a member of the fake Destiny’s Child-esque girl group Gemini’s Twin. 

The South African native noticed that as rehearsal went on, her singing role diminished until she eventually just became the faux group’s eye candy.

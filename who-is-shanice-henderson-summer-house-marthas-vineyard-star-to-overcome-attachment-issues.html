Who is Shanice Henderson? 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard' star to overcome 'attachment issues'

Publish date: 2024-03-16

CAPE COD, MASSACHUSETTS: Shanice Henderson will make her reality television debut on Bravo's 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard.' The show is a spin-off of Bravo's original 'Summer House.' According to the trailer, fans of black entertainment will be in for a treat when the series premieres on May 7, 2023. So, before the series premieres, let's get to know Shanice a little better.

The new show has an all-Black ensemble vacationing at Martha's Vineyard, a Massachusetts island south of Cape Cod that was one of the first beach places where African-Americans could vacation and buy property. Nicholas "Nick" Arrington, Jasmine Ellis Cooper, Silas Cooper, Jordan Emanuel, Bria Fleming, Amir Lancaster, Jason Lyke, Preston Mitchum, Summer Marie Thomas, and Alex Tyree round out the cast.

Who is Shanice Henderson?

Shanice Henderson is described by Bravo as "the quintessential party girl" who "loves to have fun and knows the hottest spots in town." Shanice is originally from Phoenix, Arizona, but her life adventure has brought her to plenty of places for business and leisure, providing her with a variety of life experiences that she enthusiastically shares on social media.

Shanice is 31 years old, according to her Instagram feed. Her birthday was recently celebrated by the 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard' ensemble. She also celebrated her 31st birthday with an Instagram post, disclosing her birthdate as April 8, 1992. While her previous relationship left her heartbroken, she is healing and ready to return to the dating scene. When it comes to dating, she maintains her high criteria – men must be tall and athletic.

Shanice Henderson and her co-stars worked as Playboy bunnies

Shanice may have earned a reputation as a party girl, but her professional background includes several years of expertise in a variety of fields. While she is best known for her time as a Playboy Club bunny, she has held a variety of office-related jobs in the past and recently.

Shanice works as an Assistant at Lifesource Irrigation Inc, according to her Harri profile. Shanice has worked for the organization since August 2016, and she lives in New York City. Shanice formerly worked as a Mortgage Specialist at Chase Mortgage from August 2015 until July 2016. Shanice's prior job included a brief spell as a Receptionist/assistant at Primelerner in 2015.

Previously, in December 2013, she took on hosting responsibilities at Twin Peaks Scottsdale, which was her first actual professional experience and lasted a whole year. Shanice will take on another professional route in entertainment after participating in Season 1 of the Bravo spin-off series 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard.' She has all it takes to thrive in her new job.

With over 500 Instagram posts and various tales shared with her 15.7k Instagram followers, the reality star has a very active Instagram presence. Shanice has one of the most followers of the 'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard' cast and will be the center of attention when the show premieres.

Furthermore, her days as a Playboy Club bunny are only the tip of the iceberg in terms of everything that makes Shanice the epitome of fun and entertainment. Surprisingly, Shanice and her co-stars Bria, Jordan, and Jasmine all worked as Playboy Club bunnies during the day, where they formed a long bond. It would be interesting to see how the quartet's relationship develops and how they interact with the other cast members when the show actually begins.

'Summer House: Martha's Vineyard' premieres on May 7 at 9/8c on Bravo.

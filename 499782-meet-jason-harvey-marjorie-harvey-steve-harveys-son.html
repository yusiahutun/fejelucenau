Early life

The fashion designer was born in 1991 in Memphis, Tennessee. He is the son of Steve Harvey and his third wife, Marjorie Harvey. However, Steve is not Jason Harvey’s biological father. Instead, his birth father is rumoured to be Jim Townsend, Marjorie's ex-husband, and a drug dealer in prison until 2017.

Jason has six siblings – Lori, Wynton, Morgan, Brandi, Karli, and Broderick Harvey. Wynton, Karli, Brandi, and Broderick are Steven Harvey’s children from his previous marriage, thus his stepsiblings. On the other hand, Morgan, Lori, and Jason are Marjorie Harvey's biological children, whom Steve adopted in 2007 after marrying Marjorie.

Not much about Jason Harvey's childhood is known, as he didn't become famous until his stepfather entered the scene. However, he was mostly raised by his mother, with his father imprisoned for most of his life.

Jason attended a military school for his freshman year of high school. He then moved to North Spring Charter High School for his sophomore and junior years before being home-schooled for his senior year.

After graduating high school, he joined Morehouse College, a liberal arts college in Atlanta, Georgia. However, he dropped out in his third year to focus on his family and business.

Career

What does Jason Harvey do for a living? Steve Harvey's stepson is a designer. He founded Yevrah, a shoe company he started in 2013 with his business partner, Erwin Sweet. The company creates designer men's and women's shoes, which are made in Brazil.

The name Yevrah is derived from "Harvey" – spelt backwards. This is perhaps a homage to Jason's stepfather, whom (together with his mother) he credits for teaching him many things about business. His parents also support his venture and often appear on the red carpet wearing his designs.

Besides shoes, Jason is also a clothing designer. In 2022, he launched a creative house, Worldly, to inspire and promote designers of colour. Soon after its launch, the company released its debut fashion line, Eden, on Hush. The line had more than 20 designs, which were created by Jason in collaboration with his designer friend, John Byrd-Olivieri.

Steve Harvey’s son is blissfully married to Amanda Harvey (formerly Amanda Hill). The couple married on August 26, 2014, after dating for about three years. But unfortunately, they have not revealed much about how they met or even their wedding.

She has also appeared in several fashion shows, such as Elie Saab and Leonard Paris. Jason and Amanda Harvey have been married for over eight years and have four children. Their firstborn, Rose Harvey, was born in 2014.

They also have two sons, Noah Ellington (born in 2015) and Ezra Nehemiah (born in 2016). In 2020, they welcomed another daughter, Joey Iris.

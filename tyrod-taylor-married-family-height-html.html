Family, Height, Weight

The new Los Angeles Chargers quarterback was born on 3 August 1989 in Hampton, Virginia to parents Rodney and Trina Taylor. Tyrod is the only child of his parents in a small family of three.

As a single child, Tyrod attended Hampton High School. There, he learned to play quarterback, safety and returned to kickoffs for his team. Soon, he made the transition to Virginia Tech College in 2008 and became the ACC Championship Game MVP.

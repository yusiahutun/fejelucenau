Watching Angelina Jolie morph into Disneys Maleficent is mesmerising

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Disney’s Maleficent sequel is arguably the most anticipated film release this October and, being the month of Halloween, offers the ultimate costume inspiration for those wanting to marry villainous with glamorous.

Maleficent: Mistress of Evil sees Angelina Jolie return in the title role sporting twisted horns, sharp cheek prosthetics and a slash of red lipstick. The transformation, which took an hour-and-a-half on set every day, can be seen in full in this timelapse video shared by MAC. It's mesmerising:

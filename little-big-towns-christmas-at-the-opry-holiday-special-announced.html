Little Big Towns Christmas At The Opry Holiday Special Announced

Publish date: 2024-06-11

Little Big Town (Photo Courtesy: AlanWeinerPhotography.com)

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) –  Celebrating their 25th anniversary as one of country music’s most successful groups, multi-platinum Grammy-Award-winning four-piece Little Big Town – comprised of Karen Fairchild, Kimberly Schlapman, Phillip Sweet, and Jimi Westbrook – will be getting their very first holiday special on NBC this year! Announced today in NYC, “Little Big Town’s Christmas at The Opry” will be a festive 2-hour musical extravaganza from Nashville’s iconic Grand OIe Opry, featuring performances from the band alongside many exciting special guests. More exciting news from the band is coming soon!

Earlier this year, Little Big Town joined forces with longtime friends and collaborators Sugarland to deliver a stunning performance of their new song “Take Me Home” at the CMT Music Awards. The two powerhouse groups will embark on a fall U.S. tour titled Take Me Home Tour.

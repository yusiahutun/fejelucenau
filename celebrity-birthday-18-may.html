Celebrity birthdays 18 May: Tina Fey and Teresa Giudice

Publish date: 2024-05-01

Happy Birthday to actress Tina Fey and reality TV star Teresa Giudice. Both famous faces all share a star sign – Taurus, whose typical traits include reliability, practicality and ambition. To celebrate their big day, we are taking a closer look at the lives of the birthday duo...

Tina Fey turns 48

American actress and comedian Tina Fey will have a lot to celebrate this year, after the Broadway show Mean Girls, which she wrote, received 12 Tony Awards nominations. Tina, herself, received a nod for best book (IE script) of a musical. The star, who has two daughters with husband Jeff Richmond, will soon be embarking on another exciting project: filming with superstar Nicki Minaj. The artist, 35, announced the news on her Instagram, telling her 86 million fans that the new film will be titled Get Ya Life Ugly.

Teresa Giudice turns 46

The Real Housewives of New Jersey star has definitely gotten her life back on track after being released from prison in 2015. Teresa began her jail sentence on 5 January 2015 after pleading guilty to 41 counts of fraud and although she was sentenced to 15 months in a federal prison, she was released later in December. Currently, the mother-of-three has been busy promoting her book, Standing Strong, around the country and attending social events. Most recently, she was matron of honour at her co-star Danielle Staub's wedding in the Bahamas. Teresa's husband Joe is currently serving a 41-month prison sentence after pleading guilty to conspiracy and bankruptcy fraud.

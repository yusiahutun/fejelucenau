9 Sexiest Tattoos in Country Music

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Tattoos may not be as prominent in country as they are in other genres, but some of our favorite country artists have some seriously sexy ink marks! We've narrowed down the best of the body art to this 9 Sexiest Tattoos in Country Music list.

Brantley Gilbert is covered in tattoos, so you know he made the cut. Although it was hard to narrow his down, we picked one area that really appeals to our eyes. He's not the only male artist to be found — three other male acts make the list for forearm ink and a couple of bicep tatts.

And when it comes to country's leading ladies, there's no shortage of sexy ink! Five make our lineup, including Maren Morris' new Texas-inspired tattoo. Its gorgeous color and perfect placement made it a no-brainer for one of the sexiest. Kacey Musgraves and Cassadee Pope's feminine body art make an appearance on here, too.

Armani White Stated That His Music Feels Similar To Billie Eilishs Music

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Armani White, the Content Creator of TikTok and a rapper, has signed a contract with hip-hop music publishing firm Def Jam Records. This summer, the singer rose to prominence with his song Billie Eilish, which he dedicated to the Grammy-winning artist. According to White, the song is mostly about Billie Eilish’s style and the music she makes. Def Jam Records has also collaborated with some of the most well-known artists, including Jay-Z, Nas, and Kanye West.

Armani White compares his music to that of Billie Eilish.

The 25-year-old Philadelphia musician’s song, which has received over 80 million views on TikTok, has audiences nodding. Many of Eilish’s tracks include legendary moments recreated by White. He claims that he attempts to convey the same emotions as Eilish conveys in her song. They described the resemblance as follows:

“I mean, honestly, I believe the song’s about Billie Eilish’s aesthetic, but it’s also about the character that Billie Eilish’s music represents.” I feel like it’s similar to what I try to convey in my songs.”

While discussing his work with Def Jam Records, Armani White said that he considers himself “fortunate” to have people that trust in him. He stated:

“It’s a privilege to work with folks that just trust in me and understand the culture.” They understand who I am and who I am trying to be, which I believe is quite essential.”

He went on, saying:

“And particularly with such a prominent label as Def Jam.” I mean, Def Jam has clearly just built up some of the biggest names in hip-hop from the start.”

When the Party’s Over is the rapper’s favorite album by Eilish, and she approves of his song about her. Tom Brady of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers also shared his song. Brady shared the viral song on his Instagram story.

The Philadelphia rapper maintains that he will continue to make songs for other superstars. Diamond Dallas, one of his most recent singles, was inspired by Diamond Dallas Page, a former professional wrestler who once wrestled in WWE.

More details on Armani White

Armani White, a 25-year-old West Philadelphia rapper, has been writing songs since he was eight years old. The musician described an early domestic abuse incident between his parents as a watershed moment in his life. He claims that since his parents disregarded his shouts throughout the battle, urging them to stop, he now turns his rage into music. The singer-songwriter said that he was influenced by B.O.B’s mixtape B.O.B. vs. Bobby Ray and several Kanye West tracks.

Stick Up, his 2015 hit, garnered him enough prominence to put him on the map in the music business. He was even named a “Artist to Watch in 2015” by MTV Wrap Up. He subsequently collaborated with Big Sean, GoldLink, and Big K.R.I.T. to make new songs, but he took a two-year hiatus when his father died. In 2017, he released a song named Casablanco and Public School. His father and the life he lived motivated him to attend Public School. In 2018 and 2019, the singer released two singles. The majority of his music can be heard on his YouTube channel, which has over 57k subscribers.

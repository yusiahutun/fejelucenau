William Ruto Praises Aden Duale During Launch of His Book: &quot;Straight Shooter&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-30
  • Defence CS Aden Duale released his new book For The Record in a posh event held at the Serena Hotel on the evening of May 11
  • President Ruto heaped praises on his defence CS for releasing the new book, which he said had proved that Duale is a straight shooter
  • The head of state said it was also the story of Duale and his life in public service since he started out

President William Ruto has praised Defence Cabinet Secretary Aden Duale during the launch of his hard-hitting book For the Record.

Duale's book was launched today at the Serena Hotel in Nairobi, and the head of state was among the high-profile dignitaries in attendance.

Taking to his social media pages, Ruto heaped praises on his defence CS for releasing the new book, which he said had proved that Duale is a straight shooter.

"The book recounts a journey that begins in Garissa and ends at the heart of Kenya's politics covering all major political events of the last two decades, including the International Criminal Court cases, the rise and implosion of Jubilee Party, the 2013 and 2017 elections and the Kenya Kwanza victory of 2022," he said.

Ruto said that in as much as For the Record brings to light the politics of betrayal and deceit that played out after the 2017 elections, it was also the story of Duale n and his life in public service.

"It is the story of a man and his life in public service, a story that is forged in the heat of Garissa Town and deep lifelong faith in Kenya and Allah," he said.

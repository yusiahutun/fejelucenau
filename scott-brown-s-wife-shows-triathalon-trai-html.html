Scott Brown's wife shows off her toned physique in a blue swimsuit

Publish date: 2024-06-09

Former Massachusetts Senator Scott Brown's wife Gail Huff is showing off her athletic figure at age 54. 

The TV journalist and former model posted a picture of herself in a blue swimsuit to Facebook on Tuesday, writing 'training for swim and bike race!'. As of Wednesday, the picture has received over 500 likes. 

When her husband was elected to the U.S. Senate in 2010, a lot of attention was paid to his 1982 nearly-nude spread in Cosmopolitan magazine. 

Gail Huff, the wife of former Massachusetts Senator Scott Brown, has showed off her figure in a photo posted to Facebook on Tuesday

Athletic Brown has continued to maintain his physique and so has his wife is appears. The two met through their past modelling careers. 

In an interview with the Boston Herald, Huff said that the picture was taken by her husband while they were training in Aruba. 

'I’m just starting back to racing and I’m not very good. ... Scott’s a real triathlete. I’m a wannabe,' Huff said. 

However, she went on to say that she hopes the picture inspires other women to keep competing even into middle age.  

The TV journalist, pictured with her former senator husband in 2011, says she is training for relay races and a triathalon

'I wasn’t sure serious athletic competing was a reality once you passed 50, but I’m giving it a try!' she said. 'I was a competitive swimmer and diver up until college. But I’m stronger and faster now than I was then ... so why not? I see a lot of women going back to competing after their kids are raised and they have more time on their hands. That’s me!' 

Huff says that she recently ran a 5K road race, and plans to ride in a 50-mile bike race with her husband this weekend to raise money for the American Lung Association.

Amazons 8 Top-Selling Retinol Skincare Products Start at $8

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Whether you’re a skincare aficionado or just dipping your toes into the space, you’ve probably heard of retinol. The powerful vitamin A variant is known for its anti-aging benefits, like softening fine lines and evening out skin tone and texture, which explains why many of Amazon’s most popular skincare products contain retinol as a main ingredient. 

Below, I rounded up eight customer-loved retinol serums and moisturizers from top brands like CeraVe, RoC, L'Oréal Paris, and Neutrogena. The best part? Everything costs $25 or less, with prices starting at just $8. So, if you’ve been meaning to add a retinol-infused product into your skincare routine, this is your sign to take the plunge. And as always — be sure to wear SPF, as retinol can make your skin more sensitive to the sun.  

Best Retinol Skincare Products on Amazon

CeraVe Resurfacing Retinol Serum

The CeraVe Resurfacing Retinol Serum is like a “magic eraser for hyperpigmentation,” according to a reviewer. In addition to retinol, the serum is made with niacinamide to fade dark spots, licorice root extract to brighten the complexion, and ceramides to moisturize and protect the skin barrier. For the best results, the brand recommends applying a layer of the serum to your skin after cleansing and before using your moisturizer. And with 70,000 purchases in the past month alone, per Amazon, the serum must really be doing its thing. 

Advanced Clinicals Retinol Cream

Another $18 retinol cream, the Advanced Clinicals formula stands out, since you can use it on both your face and body. Plus, it's infused with soothing ingredients, like green tea extract and chamomile to calm inflammation and protect the skin from toxins, as well as aloe vera to lock in moisture and hydration. A shopper even confirmed it “goes on smooth, smells good, [and is] helping with fine lines and stretch marks.” Sign us up. 

Good Molecules Gentle Retinol Cream

At just $8 — the cheapest product on the list — the Good Molecules Gentle Retinol Cream is “perfect for beginners or those with younger or sensitive skin,” per a reviewer. The retinol cream is suitable for all skin types, according to the brand, and only requires a pea-sized amount for powerful results. It’s made with grapeseed oil for intense hydration, allantoin to soothe inflammation, and of course, retinol to smooth fine lines and wrinkles. Shoppers claim it’s left them with “taught, glowy skin” and “instantly removed fine lines off [their] forehead.” Despite its low price tag, the cream “really works,” a shopper said.

Lorde VMA MTV Awards Performance 2017

Publish date: 2024-02-27

When Lorde hit the stage at tonight's MTV VMAs, viewers became instantly confused when she performed an interpretive dance instead of singing during her song "Homemade Dynamite." As it turned out, she was unable to actually sing due to a bad case of the flu, so instead gave her best dance moves in a Jourden x Nike silver metallic dress, Champion sweatpants, and Adidas sneakers.

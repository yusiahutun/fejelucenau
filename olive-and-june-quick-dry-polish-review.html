Olive and June Quick Dry Polish Launch 2023 Review

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Whether you've become your own nail tech since the start of the pandemic, or you frequent your local salon for a regular mani and pedi, there's one thing we can all agree on: waiting for your polish to dry is the worst.

To make sure all your hard work and patience doesn't go to waste with an accidental smudge, the nail care experts at Olive & June have just launched their new anti-chip, Quick Dry polish. Available in 19 shades plus a top coat, the collection features a new formula that lasts for up to five days per wear, and dries in just a minute flat.

Like all Olive & June nail polishes, their latest collection is vegan and 7-Free, and retails for $7.50 each. If you're looking to upgrade your nail essentials, you can also add up to six bottles of your favorite Quick Dry polish to the brand's Mani System for $85. And to round out your overall polish collection, the full 19-shade range, plus a Quick Dry top coat, is available for $150.

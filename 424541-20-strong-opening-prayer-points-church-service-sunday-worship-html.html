Opening prayer for worship service

Prayers for worship service allows worshippers to have an opportunity to express their love to God through praise, worship, prayers and teachings of the Bible. Having an opening prayer is an effective way to invite God's presence in the service. The following are sample opening prayers for a worship service.

Father, we worship you. Thank you go gathering us and helping us to know your word. As we gather here, we prohibit all forces of the evil one in our midst. The Bible says whatever we refuse on earth will be forbidden in heaven, and whatever we permit on earth shall be allowed in heaven. Dear God, we let your Holy Spirit get into this place to help us know you better. Build this church on a foundation that cannot be shaken as you promise in your word. In Jesus name, we pray, Amen.

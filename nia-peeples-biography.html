Who is Nia Peeples?

Los Angeles-born Nia Peeples is an actress and a singer. Presently, she is famous for acting in a favorite drama show, “Pretty Little Liars.”

Additionally, she is also a famous actress for playing Karen Taylor in the TV series, “The Young and the Restless.” As a singer, she has been involved in R&B and dance music.

Her released albums are “Nothin’ But Trouble,” “Nia Peeples,” and “Songs of the Cinema.”

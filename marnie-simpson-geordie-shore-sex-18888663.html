Geordie Shore star claims PORN SOUNDS were played over her sex scenes

Publish date: 2024-05-23

Marnie, 26, is famous for her racy clinches on MTV shows Geordie Shore and Single AF.

But when she watches them back she gets a saucy surprise.

Marnie told the Daily Star Sunday: “MTV put sound effects on when they show the bits where I’m having sex. I remember one time I was watching Geordie Shore and it got to a bit where I was having sex with Aaron Chalmers.

“I was thinking, ‘That noise isn’t me…’ They must have taken the noises from a porno. They weren’t noises I’d ever make during sex.”

Marnie has now left Geordie Shore, two months after the Daily Star Sunday exclusively revealed she wanted to quit.

But the sex bomb will be back on our screens later this month for the new series of Ex On The Beach.

Marnie jetted to Marbella to film the show and when she rekindled her romance with Casey Johnson she couldn’t resist more sex on camera.

She said: “We went all the way while we were filming. It’s all just fun.

“I used to sometimes get embarrassed about what was shown on Geordie Shore, but that was only because of the sound effects. But I won’t when this airs.

“If you sign on the dotted line to do a reality show then you’re going in there to do reality. And in reality, if you like someone and you’re getting on, then sex happens.”

Ex On The Beach returns March 20 on MTV.

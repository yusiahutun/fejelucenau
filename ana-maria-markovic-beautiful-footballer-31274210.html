'World's most beautiful footballer' asks fans if she's the hottest as they dub her 'GOAT'

Publish date: 2024-05-12

Ana Maria Markovic has been asking her fans on social media for their opinion about whether she is the 'world's most beautiful footballer'.

The 23-year-old Croatian international, who plays her football in Switzerland for Grasshopper, has gained a significant social media following thanks to her stunning uploads. The blonde forward routinely wows her three million Instagram, followers with snaps of herself having fun on holiday, or enjoying training.

Recently, she dropped some stunning Paris photos for her followers to enjoy which left some of them asking whether they could marry her.

Space NK Shimmering Spice Candle

Space NK Shimmering Spice Candle

Space NK has officially signalled the start of the festive season: Shimmering Spice has arrived. Continually restocked (due to remarkable demand) from now until the big day, this timeless blend of sweet, warming spices and grounding cedar never fails to spark joy.

Trudon Scented Altair Candle

Trudon Scented Altair Candle

As the oldest candlemaker in the world, Cire Trudon – which is celebrating its 380th anniversary this year – knows exactly how to craft a centrepiece-worthy vessel. This year's divine Christmas offering comprises four limited-edition candles, each one hand-painted with gold leaf, which shines through the votive once the wick is lit.

The scent of the season? It has to be the new Altaïr, which combines spicy rose with powerful oud, offering something a little left of the traditional Christmassy notes.

Diptyque Coton Candle

Diptyque Coton Candle

Diptyque's hugely coveted Christmas candles have arrived, and this year's are every bit as beautiful as you've likely come to expect.

The extra-special festive collection features three modern scents. Sapin is the bracing, mind-clearing choice, while Delice is a decadent ode to sugar and spice. Our choice, however, has to be the shimmering white musk notes of Coton. The icing on the cake? Each one comes with its own gold metal lid to keep the wax protected between burns.

Bamford Geranium Christmas Candle

Bamford Geranium Christmas Candle

The signature scent of Bamford's heavenly spas comes wrapped up in special packaging this year – ideal for anyone who wants a festive aesthetic without the seasonal scent.

Cloon Keen Noble Fir Candle

Cloon Keen Noble Fir Candle

Born on the coastline of Galway, independent perfumery Cloon Keen creates small-batch artisanal candles that look every bit as beautiful as they smell. A limited-edition festive offering, Noble Fir is a potent blend of pine needles, cedar and amber. The silvery votive is weighty and larger than most, and will glow softly once you light the wick.

Floris Cinnamon and Tangerine Candle

Floris Cinnamon and Tangerine Candle

From the brand that famously made the late Queen's perfume of choice, this festive candle stays true to tradition with an aroma of sweet, spiced cinnamon and juicy tangerine.

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Mon Beau Sapin Candle

Maison Francis Kurkdjian Mon Beau Sapin Candle

Trust Francis Kurkdjian to turn the traditional scents of Christmas into something truly indulgent. The renowned perfumer's ode to the Christmas tree is a nose-twitching take on pine that evolves into a resinous, woody aroma that smells spookily just like the real thing.

The White Company Winter Signature Candle

The White Company Winter Signature Candle

The White Company has been creating festive candles in its signature Winter scent for over two decades, with a host of loyalists snapping them up each year.

Burning with a traditional blend of clove, cinnamon and sweet orange, it's easy to understand the cult status.

Rosa Bianca Salazar (; born July 16, 1985) is an American actress. She had roles in the NBC series Parenthood (2011–2012) and the FX anthology series American Horror Story: Murder House (2011). She made her breakthrough as the title character of the film Alita: Battle Angel (2019) and is best known for starring in the series Brand New Cherry Flavor (2021), which she also co-produced.

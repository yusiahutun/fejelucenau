See Jennifer Lopez's Spring-Ready Cropped Turtleneck

Publish date: 2024-02-08

Getting dressed for spring weather isn't as straightforward as you might think. It's almost always cold in the morning, but by the time afternoon rolls around, it can be sunny and 65 degrees. When you factor in the rain and humidity, it becomes a math problem too complex to solve. But fear not, because Jennifer Lopez has the answer: the cropped turtleneck. Much like the sleeveless turtleneck, the cropped version keeps you cool while also looking hot. 

On Sunday, J.Lo stepped out for lunch at Sadelle's in New York City and showed off her impeccable abs in the aforementioned turtleneck top in a deep shade of emerald. On the bottom, she kept it casual with a pair of baggy, medium-wash jeans that featured a row of buttons down the front in lieu of a zipper, as well as brown suede platform boots. She accessorized with a Lady Dior bag that matched the green color of her sweater and aviator sunglasses in the shape of octagons.   

Her light brown hair was worn down in voluminous curls with a middle part, while a pink glossy lip and her signature J.Lo glow finished off her beauty look. 

Later that day, Lopez celebrated the launch of her new Intimissimi campaign at the brand's flagship store in Midtown Manhattan, swapping her jeans and boots for an ultra-glam minidress and heels.

At the event, the multi-hyphenate showed off her fashion range in a pink, sequin-embellished dress with an itty-bitty hemline, a high neck, and long sleeves. Matching baby pink pumps, mermaid waves, and a red lip provided the final touches to her look.

Elle Duncan career

Elle Duncan began her career as an intern back at home in Atlanta on the 2 Live Stews, an American sports syndicated radio show, also known as The Zone. She was later hired by radio veteran Ryan Cameron on V-103 and joined the "Ryan Cameron Show." There she worked as an on-air personality and traffic reporter. In 2009, she went on to host an afternoon show for the radio station. After some time, she took on the morning show "Frank and Wanda" which focused on sports reporting.

Elle learned a lot from Ryan, especially the art of keeping the audience interested and engaged. He also helped her get a job as a sideline reporter for the Atlanta Hawks. She also contributed to pre and post-game shows for the Atlanta Falcons radio network. In 2012 her passion for sports reporting became very evident when she joined 11 Alive, NBC Affiliate WXIA-TV. The network didn’t assign her the role of a sports reporter but rather a traffic reporter.

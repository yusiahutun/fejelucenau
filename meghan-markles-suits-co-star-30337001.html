Meghan Markle's Suits co-star Max Beesley 'terrified' after being robbed at gunpoint

Publish date: 2024-04-12

Telly actor Max Beesley was robbed at gunpoint.

The actor, 52, got the shock of his life when he was targeted while out in London.

It was years ago but he still remembers the incident vividly.

He said: “I remember years ago I got robbed in Ladbroke Grove at gun point. I was in a chair with a gun at the back of my head.”

Max was so terrified by the gun he was unable to move.

The star was shocked by how he reacted.

He said: "I remember thinking if that ever happened, I’ll go to work and I’ll take care of business. And I completely froze.

"Even if I’d had a weapon,'I’d have said, 'Brother I’ve got a gun here, man, do you want to take it'’. I was terrified."

Max had a stark reminder of the incident while filming his new drama Hijack.

The series, which is streaming in AppleTV+, is about armed terrorists taking over a plane.

It comes after Max refused to answer question about his former Suits co-star Meghan Markle during an appearance on Good Morning Britain on June 27.

Pointing out that he hadn't had much experience working with her, as he had only appeared in a few episodes of Suits, he described her as a "great actress" before awkwardly refusing to discuss her further.

However, following talk of his latest on-screen venture, host Ed Balls asked: "One more chance... Meghan Markle?"

Dodging this question, Max instead gestured at the table in front of them, which was covered in newspapers, exclaiming: "What a lovely array of newspapers you've got here!"

Viewers at home were rather critical of presenters Ed and Susanna Reid's decision to ask Max about Meghan, with one tweeting: "Really? You’ve got @maxbeesley7 there & you’re still obsessed with Meghan Markle? Well done Max for not answering."

"Well done to @maxbeesley7 for sidestepping the questions on Meghan Markle. You clearly looked uncomfortable but dealt with it well" added another.

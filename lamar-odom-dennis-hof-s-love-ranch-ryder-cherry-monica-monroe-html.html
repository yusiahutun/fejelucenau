During Odom's time at the brothel, he was drinking cognac and taking herbal Viagra, although there were no signs he had been using any other drugs. 

A source for E! News told the site that 'virtually every drug imaginable was found in his system' when Odom was taken to the hospital.

Sources added to TMZ that the mucus in Odom's lungs was caused by smoking crack-cocaine and that doctors also found opiates in his system, as well as needle marks on one of his arms. This counters previous statements made by Dennis Hof, the brothel proprietor, who was quick to say that none of his girls saw the athlete taking illicit drugs during his stay.

However, the E! source says it appears the overdose was not intentional but possibly a result of Odom's history of drug abuse.

'The long term effects of his drug use have taken a toll on his body and the binge this time was too much to handle,' the source said. 

In a FAQ page on the ranch's website, the proprietors admit to hosting so-called 'Viagra parties', which are known to include party drugs like ecstasy and cocaine used along with the erectile dysfunction pill.

The ranch is clear, however, that they do no provide Viagra and that drugs are absolutely never allowed. 

Hof said: 'It's terrible. You got people, the girls that found him, out here that are very upset.

The 68-year-old Arizona native bought the Love Ranch in 2010, Las Vegas Weekly reported. 

He owns nine brothels and a strip club in Nevada, according to CNBC.  

In addition to a VIP section, the newly renovated Love Ranch features a bar and massage rooms.

The Love Ranch and Hof's other brothels also have Jacuzzi rooms, hot tubs, and water rooms.

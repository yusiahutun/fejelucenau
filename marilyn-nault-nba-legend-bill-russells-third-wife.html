Marilyn Nault Early life

The jewelry salesperson was born in Concordia, Cloud County, Kansas, United States on January 15th, 1960. She is the daughter of Quentin and Helen Nault and has four brothers; Phil, Eric, James, and Kyle. Growing up she was surrounded by her siblings as they were all brought up together in one household.

The loss of her father in 1987 was a great tragedy for the family, as he had owned a jewelry store in Kansas. Even more sadly, one of her brothers passed away several years after her death in 2020.

Nault was brought up in a devoutly religious family, with her parents adhering to the Catholic belief system. She went to a Catholic high school located in Kansas and decided against pursuing higher education, instead opting for employment after graduating from high school.

Career

After completing her high school education, Nault relocated to Seattle and followed in her father’s footsteps by taking a job as a jewelry salesperson. It was through this position that she met the love of her life, Bill Russell; they first crossed paths at one of the jewelry stores where Nault worked in the city.

Nault had a multifaceted professional career prior to her relationship with Bill Russell; she worked as a jewelry salesperson and also served as his secretary, providing assistance in accounting. After they began dating and eventually married, Nault decided to focus solely on being a housewife instead of continuing her work outside the home.

Marilyn Nault and Bill Russell enjoyed a private marriage, rarely participating in public events. When they did appear together, it was usually at basketball games or dinner engagements. Unfortunately, after just over 10 years of marriage, the relationship was tragically cut short when Nault passed away in 2009.

Children

Nault and Bill Russell did not have any biological children together, however, Nault was a stepmother to the three children from Russell’s first marriage; Karen Kenyatta Russell, William Jr., and Jacob. She provided them with love and support as if they were her own.

Bill Russell married Jeanine Russel sometime around 2018, following the death of his third wife, Marilyn Nault. This was his fourth marriage and lasted until he passed away in 2022. Prior to this union with Jeanine and Marilyn Nault, Bill had two other wives.

Russell’s first marriage was to Rose Swisher, his college sweetheart, in 1956. However, the relationship gradually deteriorated over time and they divorced in 1973.

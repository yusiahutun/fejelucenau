Newark boy, 3, is FOUND unharmed 'after being abducted by man in silver Hyundai', sparking Amber Ale

Publish date: 2024-04-30

A three-year-old boy has been found after he was reportedly abducted and an Amber alert was issued.

Pedro DaSilva was taken from Newark, New Jersey on Tuesday morning, police said. 

Police previously said they were searching for a white male suspect who fled in a silver Hyundai Sonata with license plate number S27-SLD.

He was described as wearing a black North Face jacket, black pants, and white sneakers.

A photo of the boy was not released.

Fans were quick to send their birthday wishes in the comments, and on top of that, called out how amazing she looks in the photo. “Pow! Angela previously celebrated her 60th birthday in early July with 40 of her friends and loved ones in NYC — and to no surprise, it was a great time! “Throughout the night, people kept on saying, ‘Angela throws the best parties!'” an insider exclusively told Closer Weekly of the birthday bash.

On top of that, Angela is focused on her beaming career (she previously starred in Mission: Impossible — Fallout and Black Panther, and her show, 9-1-1, was picked up for a second season). “If you’re passionate, you have a great shot at being successful,” she told Closer. “I’ve learned to always keep my head up.” 

“I’m living my dream,” Angela continued. “I’ve come to understand that I possess a strength that people can feel and appreciate, a strength and sensitivity. I feel very blessed.” What an inspiration!

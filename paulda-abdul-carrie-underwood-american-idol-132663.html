Their big reunion comes after the exciting news that American Idol is coming back to TV after leaving for what seemed to be forever last spring. The show originally appeared on FOX for 15 seasons, but it will now re-air on ABC.

In a recent interview, Paula opened up about the upcoming revival of the music competition. “I’m excited for their reboot, and I think that ABC is a wonderful network for it as well,” she said. “I think it’s perfect.”

However, she doesn’t see herself being a part of it. “I had an incredible time on that show, and am blessed to have been part of it from the beginning but I think that they need to do a whole reboot,” Paula explained.

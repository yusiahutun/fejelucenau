Live Nation Announces 2015 Oddball &amp; Curiosity Comedy Tour

Publish date: 2024-04-22

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Live Nation is teaming up with comedy producers Funny Or Die to stage the 2015 Oddball Comedy and Curiosity Festival.

The festival will feature Aziz Ansari with Amy Schumer appearing as a guest on all dates.

Joining Ansari and Schumer on the Oddball stage are a rotating cast of nearly 20 additional performers including Anthony Jeselnik, Ashley Barnhill, Bridget Everett, Dave Attell, Jay Pharoah, Jeff Ross, Jim Norton,John Mulaney, Michael Che, Nick Kroll, Nick Thune, Nikki Glaser, Rachel Feinstein, Rory Scovel, Sebastian Maniscalco, Tim Minchin, T.J. Miller, and Todd Barry.

The tour will hit 18 cities in total, kicking off on Friday, Aug. 28 at Kansas City's Starlight Theatre, and culminates with a show at the Gexa Energy Pavilion in Dallas on Sunday, Oct. 18. – Staff Writers

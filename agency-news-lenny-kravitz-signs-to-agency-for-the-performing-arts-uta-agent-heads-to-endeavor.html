AGENCY NEWS: Lenny Kravitz Signs To Agency For The Performing Arts UTA Agent Heads To Endeavor

Publish date: 2024-04-11

Craig Fruin, Lenny Kravitz's manager at HK Management office, has confirmed that Kravitz has switched from Creative Artists Agency to APA. Troy Bakely is Kravitz's new responsible agent. Kravitz will be touring this year promoting his new Virgin Records release "Lenny".

UTA Agent Heads To Endeavor

Another film agent has jumped ship. United Talent Agency agent John Lesher, whose clients include directors Sydney Pollack, Paul Thomas Anderson and David O. Russell and actress Jennifer Jason Leigh, has joined rival agency Endeavor as a partner.

According to the Los Angeles Times, UTA agreed to settle his contract, which had 14 months left on it.

Just last week, CelebrityAccess reported that Endeavor talent agency partner Marty Adelstein was leaving the company to start his own multi-faceted entertainment company and become a manager (CA, February 11).

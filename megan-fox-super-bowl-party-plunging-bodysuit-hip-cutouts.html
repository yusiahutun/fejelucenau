See Megan Fox Wear a Plunging Bodysuit and Hip Cutouts

Publish date: 2024-08-17

Megan Fox showed some skin in innovative ways with her latest party look.

The Jennifer's Body actor and her fiancé Machine Gun Kelly attended Drake's Super Bowl party in Scottsdale, Arizona, last night, where they wore their signature edgy couple style. For the bash, Fox wore an all-black look comprised of a plunging strapless bodysuit and high-waisted wide-leg trousers. The outfit was elevated by several cutouts, including a trio of midriff openings under the suit's deep neckline, and large skin-baring circles at each hip.

She accessorized the look with black pointed-toe heels, hanging diamond cross earrings and layered gold necklaces that fell in between between cleavage and under the suit. She also wore the hot-pink wrist brace that she wore throughout Grammys weekend.

Meanwhile, MGK wore a long-sleeved crop top printed with a green, pink, and yellow cartoons, paired with low-rise leather pants and visible Dolce & Gabbana underwear. He completed his look with black Doc Marten boots, a fuzzy black bucket hat, and thin pink shades.

BACKGRID

Last weekend, the Transformers actor supported the musician at the Grammys, where he was nominated for his first Grammy in the Best Rock Album category for Mainstream Sellout. (The award ultimately went to Ozzy Osbourne.) Following the awards, Fox shared a heartfelt tribute on Instagram, paired with a pic of the couple in their jewel-adorned red-carpet looks.

"Congratulations on being in the very small percentage of artists who have received a Grammy nomination," she wrote to Kelly. "You have handled this process with a grace and maturity that I haven’t seen from you before and I’m so proud of you. Watching you walk in humility and gratitude, watching you grow into yourself and become a better man is an immeasurably more satisfying experience than watching you accept an award."

"Although those will come… and this is irrelevant I guess but I will just never ever get over how beautiful your face is. I hope one day you’ll see yourself the way I see you. I love you and I’ll keep this memory of you forever," she wrote.

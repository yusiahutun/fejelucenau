Olivia Rodrigo Takes Tokyo in a Cherry Red Heart-Printed Tube Dress

Publish date: 2024-05-01

Olivia Rodrigo’s outfits have been running the internet lately. While delivering the ultimate revenge album of the year, the pop star still has found time to both follow trends (hello, color of the season) and set new ones (underwear-as-outerwear) — and her latest look is just as charming.

On Monday, Olivia shared her OOTD from her recent travels to Tokyo in a photo dump posted to Instagram, and in the first slide of the carousel, she wore a white tube midi dress covered in cherry-red hearts. The flirty midi was strapless and featured a high slit and a delicate bow at the bodice. She kept her accessories to a minimum with a simple smattering of rings and a pair of black mule kitten heels.

Beauty-wise, she wore her raven-colored hair straight with a middle part and tucked behind her ears, while the rest of Olivia’s glam included a red lip, rosy cheeks, and subtle winged eyeliner.

“i ❤️ tokyo,” she captioned her post, which also included a photo of Japanese sweets eraser sets, a cute snap of her eating a bowl of noodles, and a picture of herself in a classic beige trench coat and a pair of black Sambas — however, instead of the red tube dress, she opted for a white tank top and black trousers underneath.

Earlier this month, Olivia celebrated the release of her sophomore album GUTS while wearing a vintage ensemble from Todd Oldham’s spring 1995 collection. For the launch party, she recreated Amber Valletta and Shalom Harlow's iconic walk-off during the brand's 1995 runway show, and combined Harlow's white cropped satin button-up and Valletta's bedazzled micro-miniskirt into one flawless look.

Woman boxer Ewa Brodnicka 'wanted by lingerie fighting boss' after weigh-in kiss

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Bosses at the Lingerie Fighting Championships (LFC) are hoping to snap up boxing stunner Ewa Brodnicka after she shared a cheeky kiss with an opponent during a recent weigh-in.

Brodnicka, a 35-year-old professional boxer, squared up to Argentine Edith Soledad Matthysse, 39, prior to their WBO world title fight on October 5 and things got heated.

During the controversial pre-fight face-off, Brodnicka planted her lips on Matthysse in a clear attempt to wind up her opponent before the main event.

Ana de Armas Debuts New Haircut After Ben Affleck Split

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Looks like Ana de Armas has got a breakup 'do.

The 32-year-old actress is making some big life changes—and her hairstyle is one of them. Following her split with Ben Affleck, the Knives Out star has allegedly been spending time in her native country Cuba, and just debuted a new look.

During a video appearance in a friend's YouTube show, the actress showed off a new chin-length bob and choppy bangs—a look that is certainly switching it up from her old haircut. Perhaps her breakup from the Gone Girl actor prompted the fresh cut?

Earlier this month, rumors of a breakup swirled before sources confirmed the split to People. The two were together for nearly a year after meeting on the set of upcoming thriller Deep Water.

"Ben is no longer dating Ana," a source told People. "She broke it off. Their relationship was complicated. Ana doesn't want to be Los Angeles based and Ben obviously has to since his kids live in Los Angeles."

Another source added that the two are just at "different points in their lives."

Affleck and de Armas were first linked in early 2020 before the COVID-19 pandemic sent the country into lockdown. The couple spent a majority of quarantine together, and just a couple of months ago, de Armas had reportedly been spending a lot of time with Affleck's kids Violet, 14, Seraphina, 11, and Samuel, 8, whom he shares with ex-wife Jennifer Garner.

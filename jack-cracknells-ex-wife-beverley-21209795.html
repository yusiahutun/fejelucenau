Jack Cracknell's ex-wife Beverley Turner finds love with younger 'domestic god'

Publish date: 2024-06-05

James Cracknell’s ex-wife Beverley Turner has found love with a “domestic god” 14 years her junior.

Beverley, who split from the Olympic rower last year, revealed the “grass is greener, softer and quieter” with her new man, eco-house developer James Pritchett.

The telly and radio presenter, who has three children with Cracknell from their 17-year marriage, gushed: “The universe has sent me a wonderful new partner via a mutual friend.

“He happens to be a domestic god 14 years my junior, who built a surprise vegetable patch in my garden when I was on holiday with the kids, created an outdoor cinema screen and replaces light bulbs on the day he notices that they’re blown. He listens when I simply need a rant.”
